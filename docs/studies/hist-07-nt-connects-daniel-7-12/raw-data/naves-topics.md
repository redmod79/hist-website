# Raw Nave's Topic Data

## ANTICHRIST
- General scriptures concerning MAT 24:5,23,24,26; MRK 13:6,21,22; LUK 21:8; 2TH 2:3-12; 1JN 2:18,22; 4:3; 2JN 1:7
- To be destroyed REV 19:20; 20:10,15

## PROPHECY
- Inspired ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
- Sure fulfillment of EZK 12:22-25,28; HAB 2:3; MAT 5:18; 24:35; ACT 13:27,29
- Cessation of LAM 2:9
- Of apostasy 1JN 2:18; JUDE 1:17,18
- False teachers 2PE 2; 3
- Tribulations of the righteous REV 2:10
- OLD TESTAMENT MESSIANIC PASSAGES QUOTED IN THE NEW TESTAMENT (extensive list including ISA 9:7 with DAN 7:14,27 with LUK 1:32,33)

## DANIEL
- A Jewish captive, also called BELTESHAZZAR
  - Educated at king's court DAN 1
  - Interprets visions DAN 2; 4; 5
  - Prophecies of DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12; MAT 24:15
  - Wisdom of DAN 1:17; EZK 28:3
  - Devoutness of DAN 2:18; 6; 9; 10; 12; EZK 14:14

## SEAL
- A stamp used for signifying documents
  - Decrees signified by 1KI 21:8; EST 8:8
  - Documents sealed with: covenants NEH 9:38; 10:1; ISA 8:16; decrees EST 8:8; DAN 6:9; deeds JER 32:10
  - Lion's den secured by DAN 6:17
  - The gravesite of Jesus MAT 27:66
  - Circumcision was regarded as a seal of righteousness ROM 4:11
- FIGURATIVE
  - Of secrecy DAN 12:9; REV 5:1
  - Of certainty of divine approval JHN 6:27; ROM 15:28; 2CO 1:22; EPH 1:13; 4:30; REV 7:3,4

  - Open door REV 4:1; Throne REV 4:2,3
  - Book with seven seals REV 5:1-5
  - Beast from sea REV 13:1-10
  - Son of man with sickle REV 14:14-16
