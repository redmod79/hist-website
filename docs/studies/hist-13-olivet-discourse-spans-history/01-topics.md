# Topics Research

## Question
Does Jesus's Olivet Discourse (Matthew 24) span from the apostolic era to the second coming? How does the dual-question structure explain both near and far fulfillment? What does "this generation" (Matt 24:34) mean? And does this prophecy provide a template for reading Revelation's seals?

## Topics

### SIGN
- A miracle to confirm faith: MAT 12:38; 16:4; 24:3,30; MRK 8:11,12; 13:4; JHN 2:11; 3:2; 4:48
- Asked for by: Abraham GEN 15:8-17; Moses EXO 4:1-9; Gideon JDG 6:17,36-40; Hezekiah 2KI 20:8; Zacharias LUK 1:18
- A token of coming events: MAT 16:3,4; 24:3
- Relevance: The disciples ask for "the sign" (to semeion) of the parousia in Matt 24:3. Signs recur throughout the discourse (24:24 "great signs and wonders"; 24:30 "the sign of the Son of man").

### GOSPEL
- Called GOSPEL OF THE KINGDOM: MAT 4:23; 24:14
- Called GOSPEL OF GOD: ROM 1:1; 15:16; 1TH 2:8
- Called GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST: MRK 1:1
- Unclassified scriptures: MAT 4:23; 11:4-6; 24:14; 28:18-20; MRK 1:14,15; 13:10; 16:15; ROM 1:16,17; 10:15-18; COL 1:5,6,23,26-29; REV 14:6,7
- Prophecies relating to: ISA 2:3-5; 9:2,6,7; 42:6,7; 52:7; 55:1-5; 60:1-22; 61:1-3; JOL 2:28-31; MAT 24:14
- Relevance: Matt 24:14 is the hinge verse — "this gospel of the kingdom shall be preached in all the world... and then shall the end come." Mark 13:10 adds "must first" (dei proton). Rev 14:6 presents the "everlasting gospel" preached to every nation as a parallel.

### PROPHETS (False)
- OT warnings: DEU 13:1-3; DEU 18:20; JER 14:13-16; JER 23:25-27,30; 1KI 22:6-12; LAM 2:14; ZEC 13:3
- NT references: MAT 7:15; 24:4-5,11,23-26; MRK 13:5-6,21-22; LUK 21:8; 2TH 2:1-12; 1JN 2:18; 1JN 4:1; 2PE 2:1; REV 13:11-14
- Relevance: Deception is the opening theme of the Olivet Discourse (24:4-5) and the intensified middle warning (24:23-26). pseudochristos (G5580) appears ONLY in the Olivet Discourse (2x total NT). pseudoprophetes (G5578) bridges Olivet and Revelation.

### TEMPLE
- Destruction foretold: ISA 66:6; JER 27:18-22; EZK 7:22,25; MAT 24:2; MRK 13:2
- Destroyed by Nebuchadnezzar: 2KI 24:13; 25:9-17
- Relevance: The discourse is triggered by Jesus's prediction of the temple's destruction (Matt 24:1-2). The disciples' first question ("when shall these things be?") refers to the temple. This creates the dual-question structure.

### ABOMINATION
- Things that are abomination to God: Idolatry DEU 7:25; 27:15; Offering children in sacrifice DEU 18:10; Sorcery DEU 18:10,11
- Relevance: The abomination of desolation (Matt 24:15) is the Daniel-specific application. shiqquts (H8251) is exclusively idolatrous (28/28 OT uses). The abomination study established the Piel/Qal distinction in Daniel and the masculine/neuter participle difference between Mark and Matthew.

### DECEPTION
- Instances: Satan GEN 3:4; Herod MAT 2:8; Pharisees MAT 22:16; Ananias and Sapphira ACT 5:1
- Relevance: planao (G4105) is the primary deception verb in the Olivet Discourse (Mat 24:4,5,11,24). It reappears in Revelation (12:9; 13:14; 18:23; 19:20; 20:3,8,10) but is absent from Rev 6:2 (first seal).

### MILLENNIUM
- General scriptures: ISA 65:17-25; ZEC 9:9,10; 14:16-21; MAT 16:18,19; REV 14:6; 20:1-15
- Relevance: The scope of the Olivet Discourse is relevant to millennial interpretations — whether the discourse covers only near events or extends to the eschaton.

## Related Topics Discovered

### GENTILES (from Nave's)
- JER 10:2,3; MAT 6:7,8,31,32; ACT 14:16; ROM 1:18-32; EPH 2:12
- Relevance: Luke 21:24 "times of the Gentiles" (kairoi ethnon) is unique to Luke's account and provides an explicit duration marker for the prophecy.

### OLIVES, MOUNT OF
- 2SA 15:30; MAT 21:1; 24:3; MRK 11:1; 13:3; LUK 19:29,37; 21:37; ACT 1:12
- Relevance: The geographical setting of the discourse (Matt 24:3; Mark 13:3). Acts 1:12 notes the ascension from the same location, connecting the Olivet Discourse to the return promise of Acts 1:11.
