# Topics Research

## Question
Do Revelation 1:7, 6:14-17, 14:14-20, and 19:11-21 describe the same event — the second coming of Christ? How do the winepress, cosmic signs, and universal visibility chains connect these passages? And is there biblical support for a separate "secret rapture" before the second coming?

## Related Topics Discovered

### Joel 3:13 — The Combined Sickle + Winepress Source
Joel 3:13 is the single OT verse combining both harvest images: "Put ye in the sickle, for the harvest is ripe: come, get you down; for the press is full." This is the source text for both Rev 14:15 (sickle) and 14:19 (winepress).

### Zechariah 14:1-5 — The LORD Coming with Saints
Zech 14:5: "the LORD my God shall come, and all the saints with thee" — combines the Lord's physical coming with cosmic upheaval (Mount of Olives cleaving). Connected to Acts 1:11 (ascension from Olivet).
