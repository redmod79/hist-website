> **Revelation 8:8-9** And the second angel sounded, and as it were a great mountain burning with fire was cast into the sea: and the **third part** of the sea became blood; And the **third part** of the creatures which were in the sea, and had life, died; and the **third part** of the ships were destroyed.

> **Revelation 8:10-11** And the third angel sounded, and there fell a great star from heaven, burning as it were a lamp, and it fell upon the **third part** of the rivers, and upon the fountains of waters; And the name of the star is called Wormwood: and the **third part** of the waters became wormwood; and many men died of the waters, because they were made bitter.

> **Revelation 8:12** And the fourth angel sounded, and the **third part** of the sun was smitten, and the **third part** of the moon, and the **third part** of the stars; so as the **third part** of them was darkened, and the day shone not for a **third part** of it, and the night likewise.

> **Revelation 8:13** And I beheld, and heard an angel flying through the midst of heaven, saying with a loud voice, Woe, woe, woe, to the inhabiters of the earth by reason of the other voices of the trumpet of the three angels, which are yet to sound!

## Systematic Trumpet-Bowl Comparison Table

| Domain | Trumpet | Effect | Bowl | Effect |
|--------|---------|--------|------|--------|
| Earth | 1st (8:7) | 1/3 trees burnt, all grass | 1st (16:2) | Sores on ALL with mark |
| Sea | 2nd (8:8-9) | 1/3 sea blood, 1/3 creatures die, 1/3 ships | 2nd (16:3) | ALL sea blood, EVERY living soul died |
| Rivers | 3rd (8:10-11) | 1/3 rivers bitter, many die | 3rd (16:4-7) | ALL rivers blood + judicial verdict |
| Sun | 4th (8:12) | 1/3 sun/moon/stars darkened | 4th (16:8-9) | Sun scorches + "repented not" |
| Darkness/Torment | 5th (9:1-11) | 5 months torment, cannot kill | 5th (16:10-11) | Total darkness on beast's seat + "repented not" |
| Euphrates | 6th (9:13-21) | 1/3 men killed + "repented not" | 6th (16:12-16) | Euphrates dried, Armageddon gathered |
| Completion | 7th (11:15-19) | Voices, wrath come, ark seen | 7th (16:17-21) | "It is done," earthquake, hail, Babylon |

**Key contrasts:**
- Fraction: 1/3 (trumpets, 13+ times) vs. total/every/all (bowls, 0 times "1/3")
- Introduction: Incense/prayers ascending (8:3-4) vs. temple closed, no entry (15:8)
- Response: "repented not" alone (9:20-21) vs. "blasphemed" + "repented not" (16:9,11,21)
- Wrath vocabulary: orge at climax (11:18) vs. thymos throughout (15:1,7; 16:1), both at 16:19
- Length: 59 verses (trumpets) vs. 21 verses (bowls) -- compressed, relentless
