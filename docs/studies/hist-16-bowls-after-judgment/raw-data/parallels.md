## Additional Parallels Identified by Direct Textual Comparison

### Lev 16:17 <-> Rev 15:8 (3 shared elements)
1. Universal exclusion: kol adam lo yihyeh / oudeis edynato
2. Sanctuary location: ohel mo'ed / naos
3. Temporal "until" limit: ad tse'to / achri telesthosin

### Exo 40:34-35 <-> Rev 15:8 (4 shared elements)
1. Glory fills: kavod YHWH male' / egemisthe ... ek tes doxes tou Theou
2. Inability to enter: lo yakol labo' / oudeis edynato eiselthein
3. Sanctuary space: ohel mo'ed / naos
4. Something fills sacred space: cloud/glory / smoke

### 1Ki 8:10-11 <-> Rev 15:8 (4 shared elements)
1. Cloud/smoke fills: he'anan male' / egemisthe kapnou
2. Glory of the LORD: kevod YHWH / doxa tou Theou
3. Inability to minister/enter: lo yaklu ... le'amod lesharet / oudeis edynato eiselthein
4. The house/temple: beit YHWH / naos

### Isa 6:4 <-> Rev 15:8 (smoke vocabulary)
- "house was filled with smoke" (ashan) -> "temple was filled with smoke" (kapnos)
- kapnos corresponds to ashan (smoke from burning), NOT to anan (cloud of theophany)

### Rev 6:14 <-> Rev 16:20 (mountain/island escalation)
- 6:14: "every mountain and island were moved (ekinethesan) out of their places"
- 16:20: "every island fled (ephygen), and the mountains were not found (ouch heurethesan)"
- Escalation: moved -> fled + not found = same event, greater intensity
