# Topics Research

## Question
What are the four beasts of Daniel 7, what is the little horn that arises among the ten, and what is the judgment scene of Daniel 7:9-14? How does this chapter expand Daniel 2's succession principle and introduce the heavenly court that both Daniel 8:14 and Revelation build upon?

## Related Topics Discovered

### JESUS, SON OF MAN
Topic not found in Nave's under this exact heading. However, Jesus uses the "Son of man" title over 80 times in the Gospels, drawing directly from Daniel 7:13. Key cross-references include:
- Matt 26:64 -- Jesus quotes Dan 7:13 before the high priest
- Mark 14:62 -- Parallel account
- Rev 1:13 -- "one like unto the Son of man" (echoing Dan 7:13)
- Rev 14:14 -- "one sat like unto the Son of man" on a white cloud
- Acts 7:56 -- Stephen sees "the Son of man standing on the right hand of God"

### JESUS, JUDGE
Not found as a separate Nave's topic. The JUDGMENT topic includes Jesus as judge: JHN 5:22 ("the Father judgeth no man, but hath committed all judgment unto the Son"); ACT 10:42; ACT 17:31; 2TI 4:1.
