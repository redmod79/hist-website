## G2064 — ἔρχομαι (erchomai) — come
**Transliteration:** erchomai | **POS:** verb | **BLB Count:** 643
**Definition:** middle voice of a primary verb; to come or go

**Revelation 2-3 Usage:**
- Rev 2:5 — ἔρχομαί σοι (present M/P indicative) — "I am coming to you" (disciplinary)
- Rev 2:16 — (same form implied) — "I will come unto thee quickly" (disciplinary)
- Rev 2:25 — ἄχρι οὗ ἂν ἥξω (heko, not erchomai) — "till I come"
- Rev 3:3 — ἥξω ὡς κλέπτης (heko) — "I will come as a thief"
- Rev 3:11 — ἔρχομαι ταχύ (present M/P indicative) — "I am coming quickly"
- Rev 3:20 — εἰσελεύσομαι (eiserchomai, future) — "I will come in to him"

**NOTE:** Rev 2:25 and 3:3 actually use heko (G2240), not erchomai. Rev 3:11 uses erchomai. The shift from disciplinary erchomai (2:5, 2:16) to eschatological erchomai/heko (3:3, 3:11) marks the transition from early to later churches.
