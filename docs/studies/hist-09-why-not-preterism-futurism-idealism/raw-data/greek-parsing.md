# Raw Greek Parsing Data

## REV 12:5 — Christ's Birth and Ascension (Aorist Verbs)

Greek Text: καὶ ἔτεκεν υἱόν, ἄρσεν, ὃς μέλλει ποιμαίνειν πάντα τὰ ἔθνη ἐν ῥάβδῳ σιδηρᾷ· καὶ ἡρπάσθη τὸ τέκνον αὐτῆς πρὸς τὸν Θεὸν καὶ πρὸς τὸν θρόνον αὐτοῦ.

Key verbs:
- **ἔτεκεν** (eteken) — τίκτω [G5088](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g5088/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Indicative 3rd Sg** — "she brought forth" (COMPLETED action — Christ's birth)
- **μέλλει** (mellei) — μέλλω [G3195](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3195/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Present Active Indicative 3rd Sg** — "is about to" (ongoing/future intention — rule nations)
- **ἡρπάσθη** (herpasthe) — ἁρπάζω [G726](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g726/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Passive Indicative 3rd Sg** — "was caught up" (COMPLETED action — Christ's ascension)

SIGNIFICANCE: Two aorist verbs (birth, ascension) bracket a present-tense "is about to rule." The birth and ascension are PAST EVENTS within Revelation's prophetic sequence. This makes pure futurism impossible (these events are already past) while the sequence continues through 1260 days (12:6), the remnant (12:17), 42 months of the beast (13:5), Three Angels (14:6-12), and the harvest/Second Coming (14:14-20).

## REV 13:18 — 666 (Psephizo / Arithmos)

Greek Text: Ὧδε ἡ σοφία ἐστίν. ὁ ἔχων νοῦν ψηφισάτω τὸν ἀριθμὸν τοῦ θηρίου· ἀριθμὸς γὰρ ἀνθρώπου ἐστίν. καὶ ὁ ἀριθμὸς αὐτοῦ ἑξακόσιοι ἑξήκοντα ἕξ.

Key words:
- **ψηφισάτω** (psephisato) — ψηφίζω [G5585](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g5585/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Imperative 3rd Sg** — "let him count/calculate"
- **ἀριθμὸν** (arithmon) — ἀριθμός [G706](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g706/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Accusative Sg M** — "number"
- **θηρίου** (theriou) — θηρίον [G2342](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2342/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Genitive Sg N** — "of the beast"
- **ἀνθρώπου** (anthropou) — ἄνθρωπος [G444](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g444/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Genitive Sg M** — "of a man"
- **ἑξακόσιοι ἑξήκοντα ἕξ** — 666

SIGNIFICANCE: "The number of a man" (arithmos anthropou) — genitive of quality. The beast has a "human number." The imperative "let him count" (psephisato) invites calculation. But the text is Greek to a Greek-speaking audience — requiring transliteration into Hebrew for the Nero identification is methodologically suspect.

## REV 1:19 — Three Temporal Categories

Greek Text: γράψον οὖν ἃ εἶδες καὶ ἃ εἰσὶν καὶ ἃ μέλλει γενέσθαι μετὰ ταῦτα.

Key verbs:
- **γράψον** (grapson) — γράφω [G1125](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1125/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Imperative 2nd Sg** — "write"
- **εἶδες** (eides) — ὁράω [G3708](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3708/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Indicative 2nd Sg** — "you saw" (PAST — things already seen)
- **εἰσὶν** (eisin) — εἰμί [G1510](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1510/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Present Active Indicative 3rd Pl** — "are" (PRESENT — things that are now)
- **μέλλει γενέσθαι** (mellei genesthai) — μέλλω + γίνομαι — **Present Active Indicative + Aorist Middle Infinitive** — "is about to come to pass" (FUTURE)
- **μετὰ ταῦτα** (meta tauta) — "after these things"

SIGNIFICANCE: Three temporal categories—past (ha eides), present (ha eisin), future (ha mellei genesthai meta tauta)—are grammatically distinct. This threefold temporal framework rules out:
- Pure futurism (which would place everything future)
- Pure preterism (which would place everything past)
- Pure idealism (which would deny temporal reference)

## REV 17:10 — Seven Kings (Present Tense)

Greek Text: οἱ πέντε ἔπεσαν, ὁ εἷς ἔστιν, ὁ ἄλλος οὔπω ἦλθεν, καὶ ὅταν ἔλθῃ ὀλίγον αὐτὸν δεῖ μεῖναι.

Key verbs:
- **ἔπεσαν** (epesan) — πίπτω [G4098](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4098/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Indicative 3rd Pl** — "have fallen" (PAST — five kings)
- **ἔστιν** (estin) — εἰμί [G1510](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1510/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Present Active Indicative 3rd Sg** — "is" (PRESENT — the one who currently IS)
- **ἦλθεν** (elthen) — ἔρχομαι [G2064](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2064/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Indicative 3rd Sg** — "has come" (negated with oupō — "has not yet come")
- **δεῖ μεῖναι** (dei meinai) — "must remain"

SIGNIFICANCE: The present tense "one is" (ho heis estin) provides a temporal anchor. At the time of writing, five kings have fallen, one currently exists, and one has not yet come. This present-tense marker grounds the vision in a specific historical moment, resisting timeless idealist reading.

## REV 1:1 — En Tachei

Greek Text: Ἀποκάλυψις Ἰησοῦ Χριστοῦ... δεῖξαι τοῖς δούλοις αὐτοῦ ἃ δεῖ γενέσθαι ἐν τάχει...

Key phrase:
- **ἐν τάχει** (en tachei) — τάχος [G5034](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g5034/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Dative Sg N** — "in quickness/shortly"
- **δεῖ γενέσθαι** (dei genesthai) — "must come to pass" — echoes Daniel 2:28 LXX (ha dei genesthai)
- **ἐσήμανεν** (esemainen) — σημαίνω [G4591](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4591/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Indicative 3rd Sg** — "signified" (communicated by signs)

## GAL 3:28 — Neither Jew Nor Greek

Greek Text: οὐκ ἔνι Ἰουδαῖος οὐδὲ Ἕλλην, οὐκ ἔνι δοῦλος οὐδὲ ἐλεύθερος, οὐκ ἔνι ἄρσεν καὶ θῆλυ· πάντες γὰρ ὑμεῖς εἷς ἐστε ἐν Χριστῷ Ἰησοῦ.

Key words:
- **οὐκ ἔνι** (ouk eni) — [G1762](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1762/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Present Active Indicative 3rd Sg** — "there is not" (emphatic denial of distinction)
- **εἷς ἐστε** (heis este) — εἷς [G1520](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1520/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} + εἰμί [G1510](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1510/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Nominative Sg M + Present Active Indicative 2nd Pl** — "you are ONE" (masculine singular "one" with plural "you are" — corporate unity)

## EPH 2:14 — Middle Wall Broken Down

Greek Text: Αὐτὸς γάρ ἐστιν ἡ εἰρήνη ἡμῶν, ὁ ποιήσας τὰ ἀμφότερα ἓν καὶ τὸ μεσότοιχον τοῦ φραγμοῦ λύσας, τὴν ἔχθραν, ἐν τῇ σαρκὶ αὐτοῦ

- **ποιήσας** (poiesas) — ποιέω [G4160](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g4160/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Participle Nom Sg M** — "having made" (completed action)
- **ἀμφότερα** (amphotera) — [G297](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g297/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — "both" (Jew and Gentile)
- **ἓν** (hen) — εἷς [G1520](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1520/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Accusative Sg N** — "one" (one entity from two)
- **μεσότοιχον** (mesotoichon) — [G3320](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3320/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — "middle wall" (hapax legomenon)
- **λύσας** (lysas) — λύω [G3089](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g3089/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Participle Nom Sg M** — "having broken down/destroyed" (completed action)

## EPH 2:15 — One New Man Created

- **καταργήσας** (katargesas) — καταργέω [G2673](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2673/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Participle Nom Sg M** — "having abolished" (completed)
- **κτίσῃ** (ktise) — κτίζω [G2936](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2936/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Aorist Active Subjunctive 3rd Sg** — "might create" (divine creative act — same verb used for creation ex nihilo)
- **ἕνα καινὸν ἄνθρωπον** — "one new man" — from the two (Jew and Gentile)

SIGNIFICANCE: All participles are aorist (completed action). Christ HAS made both one, HAS broken down the wall, HAS abolished the enmity. The verb ktizo ("create") denotes new creation, not renovation. The Israel/Gentile distinction has been dissolved into "one new man" — demolishing futurism's two-peoples theology.

## 1PE 2:9 — Royal Priesthood (Israel's Titles Applied to Church)

Greek Text: ὑμεῖς δὲ γένος ἐκλεκτόν, βασίλειον ἱεράτευμα, ἔθνος ἅγιον, λαὸς εἰς περιποίησιν...

- **γένος** (genos) — [G1085](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1085/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Nominative Sg N** — "race/generation" (chosen race)
- **βασίλειον** (basileion) — [G934](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g934/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Nominative Sg N** — "royal" (from Exo 19:6 LXX)
- **ἱεράτευμα** (hierateuma) — [G2406](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2406/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Nominative Sg N** — "priesthood" (from Exo 19:6 LXX)
- **ἔθνος ἅγιον** (ethnos hagion) — [G1484](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g1484/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} + [G40](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g40/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — "holy nation" (ethnos — the standard word for Gentile/nation — now applied to the church)
- **λαὸς** (laos) — [G2992](https://www.blueletterbible.org/lexicon/g2992/kjv/tr/0-1/){:target="_blank"} — **Nominative Sg M** — "people" (God's special people, from Exo 19:5 LXX)

SIGNIFICANCE: Peter applies Exodus 19:5-6 (Israel's Sinai covenant identity titles) directly to the largely Gentile church. The word ethnos — normally meaning "Gentile/nation" (the excluded other) — is used to call the church a "holy nation." Former outsiders now bear Israel's covenant identity.
