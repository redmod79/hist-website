# Topics Research

## Question
Do the seven seals of Revelation 6 span from the apostolic era to the second coming? How do they parallel the Olivet Discourse sequence? What does the first seal represent, how does the fifth seal prove historical duration, and how does the sixth seal describe the second coming?

## Topics

### SEAL
- A stamp used for signifying documents
  - Given as a pledge (GEN 38:18)
  - Engraved (EXO 28:11,21,36; 39:6,14,30; 2TI 2:19)
  - Decrees signified by (1KI 21:8; EST 8:8)
  - Documents sealed with: Ahab's letter (1KI 21:8); Covenants (NEH 9:38; 10:1; ISA 8:16); Decrees (EST 8:8; DAN 6:9); Deeds (JER 32:10)
  - Treasures secured by (DEU 32:34)
  - Lion's den secured by (DAN 6:17)
  - The gravesite of Jesus (MAT 27:66)
  - Circumcision regarded as a seal of righteousness (ROM 4:11)
- FIGURATIVE: Of secrecy (DAN 12:9; REV 5:1); Of certainty of divine approval (JHN 6:27; ROM 15:28; 2CO 1:22; EPH 1:13; 4:30; REV 7:3,4)

### LAMB OF GOD
- A name of Jesus (JHN 1:29; REV 6:16; 7:9,10,14,17; 12:11; 13:8; 14:1,4; 15:3; 17:14; 19:17; 21:9,14,22,23,27; 22:1,3)

### MARTYRDOM
- General scriptures concerning (PSA 44:22; MAT 10:21,22,39; 16:25; 23:34,35; 24:9; MRK 13:12; LUK 9:24; 11:50; 21:16,17; JHN 12:25; ROM 8:36; 1CO 13:3; REV 6:9-11; 11:7-12; 12:11; 16:6; 17:6)
- See PERSECUTION
- INSTANCES OF: Abel (GEN 4:3-8); Prophets killed by Jezebel (1KI 18:4,13); Zechariah (2CH 24:21,22); John the Baptist (MRK 6:18-28); Jesus; Stephen (ACT 7:58-60); James the apostle (ACT 12:2); The prophets (MAT 22:6; 23:35; ROM 11:3; 1TH 2:15; HEB 11:32-37)

### GOSPEL
- Called: GOSPEL OF THE KINGDOM (MAT 4:23; 24:14); GOSPEL OF GOD (ROM 1:1; 15:16; 1TH 2:8); GOSPEL OF CHRIST (ROM 1:16; 1CO 9:12; GAL 1:7; PHP 1:27); GOSPEL OF SALVATION (EPH 1:13); GOSPEL OF PEACE (EPH 6:15); WORD OF GOD (1TH 2:13); WORD OF LIFE (PHP 2:16)
- UNCLASSIFIED SCRIPTURES: ISA 40:8; MAT 4:23; 11:4-6; 24:14; 28:18-20; MRK 1:14,15; 13:10; 16:15; ROM 1:16,17; 10:15-18; 1CO 1:18,21,24,25; COL 1:5,6,23,26-29; 1TH 1:5; REV 14:6,7
- PROPHECIES RELATING TO: PSA 46:4; ISA 2:3-5; 9:2,6,7; 25:7; 42:6,7; 52:7; 55:1-5; 60:1-22; 61:1-3; JOL 2:28-31; MIC 4:1-7; MAT 24:14

### FAMINE
- Described (DEU 28:53-57; ISA 5:13; JER 5:17; 14:1-6; LAM 1:11,19; 4:4-10; JOL 1:17-20)
- Sent as a judgment (LEV 26:19-29; DEU 28:23,24,38-42; 1KI 17:1; 2KI 8:1; PSA 105:16; ISA 3:1-8; JER 14:15-22; EZK 5:16,17; 14:13; AMO 4:6-9; MAT 24:7; LUK 21:11; REV 6:5-8)
- FIGURATIVE (AMO 8:11) — "Not a famine of bread... but of hearing the words of the LORD"
- INSTANCES OF: In Canaan (GEN 12:10; 26:1; 2SA 21:1; 1KI 17; 2KI 6:25-29); In Jerusalem from siege (2KI 25:3; JER 52:6); In Egypt (GEN 41:53-57); Universal (ACT 11:28)

### SWORD
- Used by Gideon (JDG 7:20); Goliath (1SA 21:9); Peter (MAT 26:51; JHN 18:10)
- To be beaten into plowshares (ISA 2:4; MIC 4:3)
- Made of plowshares (JOL 3:10)
- Two-edged (HEB 4:12; REV 1:16; 2:12)
- FIGURATIVE: Of war (GEN 27:40); Of judgments (DEU 32:41; PSA 17:13; HOS 11:6; ZEC 13:7)
- SYMBOLICAL (GEN 3:24; JOS 5:13; REV 1:16)

### HORSE
- Description: Great strength (JOB 39:19-25); Swifter than eagles (JER 4:13); A vain thing for safety (PSA 33:17; PRO 21:31)
- Color of (ZEC 1:8)
- Commerce in (REV 18:13)
- SYMBOLICAL (ZEC 1:8; REV 6:2-8; 9:17; 19:11-21)

### EARTHQUAKES
- General scriptures (JOB 9:6; PSA 18:7; 46:2,3; 104:32; JER 4:24)
- As judgments (PSA 18:15; 60:2; ISA 13:13,14; 24:19,20; 29:6; NAM 1:5)
- Prophecies of (EZK 38:19; ZEC 14:4; MAT 24:7; MRK 13:8; LUK 21:11; REV 11:19)
- INSTANCES OF: At Sinai (EXO 19:18); Crucifixion (MAT 27:51); Resurrection (MAT 28:2); Philippi (ACT 16:26)
- SYMBOLICAL (REV 6:12-14; 11:13; 16:18,20)

### PESTILENCE
- Sent as a judgment (LEV 26:16,25)
