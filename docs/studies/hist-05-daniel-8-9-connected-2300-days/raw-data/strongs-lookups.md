## H2782 — חָרַץ (charats) — COMPARISON WORD
**Transliteration:** chârats
**Pronunciation:** khaw-rats
**Part of Speech:** verb
**BLB Count:** 12
**Definition:** a primitive root; properly, to point sharply, i.e. (literally) to wound; figuratively, to be alert, to decide — bestir self, decide, decree, determine, maim, move.
**Occurrences:** 14 total
- "even determined" (2x), "determined" (1x), "decreed" (1x), "are determined" (1x), "that determined" (1x)
- Used in Daniel 9:26 ("desolations are determined") and Daniel 9:27 ("that determined"), and Daniel 11:36

**Critical observation:** Daniel uses charats (H2782) in 9:26 and 9:27 when he means "determined/decreed." But in 9:24, he uses chathak (H2852) instead. If both words meant the same thing, why switch? Because chathak carries the additional "cut off" meaning that charats does not.
