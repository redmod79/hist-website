## Related Studies Referenced in PROMPT.md

### 1. churches-seals-trumpets-parallels
**Question:** Are there parallels between the seven churches, seals, and trumpets?
**Key Findings:**
- All three sequences span from apostolic era to Second Coming
- Shared vocabulary: nikao (G3528), stephanos (G4735), leukos (G3022), thlipsis (G2347), thanatos (G2288), metanoeo (G3340)
- Element-by-element correspondence: Position 1 = purity/beginning; 2 = bloodshed; 3 = corruption; 4 = darkness/death; 5 = death vs faithfulness; 6 = final crisis; 7 = conclusion/kingdom
- Three complementary perspectives: churches = internal experience, seals = cosmic significance, trumpets = divine warnings
- Chiastic framework positions sequences as parallels
- Liturgical pattern: Feast of Trumpets (warnings) precedes Day of Atonement (judgment)

### 3. rev-6-14-vs-16-20-same-event
**Question:** Is Rev 6:14 the same event as Rev 16:20?
**Key Findings:**
- Rev 6:14 (kineo G2795) and Rev 16:20 (pheugo G5343 + ouch heurethesan) describe the same event
- Different emphasis: terror vs finality; or progression within same event
- After 6th seal SC vision, narrative "rewinds"; after 7th bowl, narrative proceeds forward
- This IS recapitulation structure
- Rev 6:17 asks "who shall be able to stand?" (question); Rev 16:17 declares "it is done" (completion)
