# Topics Research

## Question
What is the biblical basis for the day-year principle? How does Daniel 9:24-27's 70 weeks prophecy prove it empirically? How does it fit Jesus's life and ministry with precision, and is there any justification for separating the 70th week from the first 69?

---

## Topics

### DAY (Nave's Topical Bible)
- **Prophetic subcategory:** 2PE 3:8; DAN 8:14; 9:24-27; 12:11,12; REV 11:3; 9:15; 12:6
- Nave's itself categorizes these time references as "Prophetic" days -- distinct from literal days. This indicates the biblical tradition recognizes prophetic time as a separate category.
- Times of adversity called "Day of the Lord": ISA 2:12; 13:6,9; 34:8; JER 46:10; LAM 2:22; EZK 30:3; AMO 5:18; JOL 2:1; OBA 1:15; ZEP 1:8,18; 2:2,3; ZEC 14:1
- Judgment called "THE DAY OF THE LORD": MAL 4:5; 1CO 5:5; 2CO 1:14; 1TH 5:2; 2PE 3:10

### COVENANT (Nave's Topical Bible)
- Sacred (JOS 9:18-21; GAL 3:15), binding (JOS 9:18-20; JER 34:8-21; EZK 17:14-18)
- **Blood of:** EXO 24:8
- **OF GOD WITH MEN:**
  - Confirmed with an oath: GEN 22:16; 26:3; PSA 89:35; 105:9; LUK 1:73; HEB 6:13,17,18
  - Everlasting: GEN 8:20-22; 9:1-17; PSA 105:8,10; ISA 54:10; 61:8
  - God faithful to: LEV 26:44,45; DEU 4:31; 7:8,9; PSA 105:8-11; 106:45; 111:5
  - **Instances with Abraham:** GEN 12:1-3; 15; 17:1-22; EXO 6:4-8; PSA 105:8-11; ROM 9:7-13; GAL 3
  - **With his people:** ISA 55:3; 59:21
  - **To be confirmed:** DAN 9:27
- **THE SECOND COVENANT:** JER 31:31-34; HEB 8:4-13; 12:18-24; 13:20

**Relevance:** The Nave's entry directly links Dan 9:27 to the covenant tradition -- "to be confirmed." This connects the 70th week's covenant confirmation to the broader biblical covenant narrative, not to a hypothetical future Antichrist treaty.

### ANOINTING (Nave's Topical Bible)
- **Consecration of kings:** Saul (1SA 9:16; 10:1), David (1SA 16:3,12,13; 2SA 2:4; 5:3), Solomon (1KI 1:39), Cyrus (ISA 45:1)
- **FIGURATIVE -- Of Christ's kingly and priestly office:** PSA 45:7; 89:20; ISA 61:1; DAN 9:24; LUK 4:18; ACT 4:27; 10:38; HEB 1:9
- **Of spiritual gifts:** 2CO 1:21; 1JN 2:20,27

**Relevance:** Nave's explicitly connects Dan 9:24 ("to anoint the most Holy") with Christ's anointing at baptism (ISA 61:1; LUK 4:18; ACT 10:38). This is the "Messiah the Prince" event of Dan 9:25.

### ARTAXERXES (Nave's Topical Bible)
1. A Persian king -- Prohibits the rebuilding of Jerusalem (EZR 4:7-24)
2. King of Persia -- Decree of, in behalf of the Jews (EZR 7; NEH 2; 5:14)

**Relevance:** Two Persian kings named Artaxerxes appear, but Nave's links the decree of EZR 7 and NEH 2 to the same king. The decree in Ezra 7 (457 BC) is the critical starting point for the 70-weeks prophecy.

### JERUSALEM (Nave's Topical Bible)
- **Prophecies against:** DAN 9:2,27; JER 9:11; 19:6,15
- **Of the destruction of:** JER 7:32-34; 26:18; 32:29,31,32; DAN 9:24-27
- **Destruction foretold by Jesus:** MAT 23:37,38; 24:15; MRK 13:14-23; LUK 19:41-44; 21:20-24
- **Prophecies of the rebuilding of:** ISA 44:28; JER 31:38-40; EZK 48:15-22; DAN 9:25; ZEC 14:8-11; 2:2-5
- **Rebuilding ordered by Cyrus:** 2CH 36:23; EZR 1:1-4
- **Rebuilt by Nehemiah under Artaxerxes:** NEH 2; 3; 4; 5; 6

**Relevance:** Nave's connects Dan 9:25 ("to restore and build Jerusalem") directly with the prophecies of rebuilding and with the historical rebuilding under Artaxerxes/Nehemiah.

### SABBATIC YEAR (Nave's Topical Bible)
- Called YEAR OF RELEASE (DEU 15:9; 31:10)
- Ordinances concerning: EXO 23:9-11; LEV 25
- Israelite bondservants set free in: EXO 21:2; DEU 15:12; JER 34:14
- Creditors required to release debtors: DEU 15:1-6,12-18; NEH 10:31
- **Punishment for violation:** LEV 26:34,35; with 26:32-41; JER 34:12-22

**Relevance:** The sabbatic year system (7-year cycles) provides the cultural and legal background for understanding "weeks of years." Israel counted time in seven-year sabbatic cycles, making "seventy weeks" = 70 x 7 years = 490 years a natural reading for a Jewish audience.

### DANIEL (Nave's Topical Bible)
- A Jewish captive, also called BELTESHAZZAR
- Prophecies of: DAN 4:8,9; 7; 8; 9; 10; 11; 12; MAT 24:15
- Devoutness of: DAN 2:18; 6; 9; 10; 12; EZK 14:14

### GOSPEL (Nave's Topical Bible)
- Called GOSPEL OF THE KINGDOM (MAT 4:23; 24:14), GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST (MRK 1:1)
- Unclassified scriptures: MRK 1:14,15 ("the time is fulfilled")
- **CALLED THE NEW COVENANT:** JER 31:31-34; HEB 7:22; 8:6-13; 9:8-15; 10:9; 12:22-24
- **Prophecies relating to:** ISA 42:6,7; 49:13; 52:7; 55:1-5; 61:1-3

**Relevance:** The Gospel entry links MRK 1:14-15 as a primary gospel text. Jesus's opening proclamation that "the time is fulfilled" is classified under the gospel proclamation, connecting the prophetic timeline directly to the arrival of the kingdom.

### GENTILES (Nave's Topical Bible)
- **Prophecies of conversion:** GEN 12:3; 22:18; 49:10; PSA 2:8; ISA 2:2-5; 9:1-7; 11:1-10; 42:1-12; 49:1,5,6; DAN 2:35,44,45; 7:13,14
- **Conversion of:** ACT 10:45; 11:1-18; 13:2,46-48; 14:27; 15:7-9; ROM 1:5-7; 9:22-30; EPH 3:1-8

**Relevance:** The turning of the gospel to the Gentiles (ACT 13:46) marks the end of the exclusive Jewish focus of the 70th week (34 AD). The prophecy "seventy weeks are determined upon THY PEOPLE" (Dan 9:24) sets a bounded period for Israel's special probation.

## Related Topics Discovered

### MESSIAH (cross-referenced from ANOINTING and DANIEL)
The term "Messiah" (H4899, mashiyach) appears in Daniel 9:25-26 and is the Hebrew equivalent of Greek "Christ" (G5547, Christos). Nave's ANOINTING entry explicitly connects Dan 9:24 with Christ's figurative anointing.

### PROPHETIC TIME (from DAY entry)
Nave's classification of DAN 8:14; 9:24-27; 12:11,12; REV 11:3; 12:6 under "Prophetic" days indicates that these time periods were understood as belonging to a distinct category of prophetic reckoning rather than literal calendar time.
