# Bible Study: Revelation 13-14 — The Beast, Three Angels, and Harvest (hist-12)

## Question
How does Revelation 13's sea beast connect to Daniel 7's four beasts and little horn? How do the three angels' messages (Rev 14:6-12) function as a time marker within the historicist framework? How does the harvest (14:14-20) connect to the second coming?

## Methodology
This study follows the investigative methodology defined in `D:/bible/bible-studies/hist-series-methodology.md`.

INVESTIGATIVE METHODOLOGY:
- You are an investigator, not an advocate. Your job is to report what the evidence says.
- Gather evidence from ALL sides. If a passage is cited by historicists, examine it honestly. If a passage is cited by preterists, futurists, or idealists, examine it honestly.
- Do NOT assume your conclusion before examining the evidence.
- Do NOT state opinions. State what the text says.
- When presenting findings, state: "The text says X" (explicit). Then state: "From this, Y interpretation infers Z" and "W interpretation infers V" (inferred).
- Never use language like "irrefutable," "obviously," or "clearly proves." Use "the text states," "this is consistent with."
- The conclusion should emerge FROM the evidence, not be imposed ON it.

## Source Restrictions
- No denominational writings.
- Permitted: Scripture, historians, commentators, lexicons, grammars, reference works.

### Focus Areas

1. **Rev 13:1-2 Composite Beast — Absorbs All Four Dan 7 Beasts (lion, bear, leopard, terrifying):**
   WHAT: The sea beast of Rev 13:1-2 combines features of all four Daniel 7 beasts in reverse order (leopard-body, bear-feet, lion-mouth) plus receives the dragon's power/seat/authority. It has seven heads and ten horns (matching the total heads of Dan 7's beasts: lion=1, bear=1, leopard=4, fourth=1 = 7 heads).
   WHY: The hist-02 study established N7 — the composite nature is observable textual fact using verbatim LXX vocabulary. This is the structural link between Daniel and Revelation that establishes continuity.
   HOW: Retrieve REV 13:1-2 with full chapter context. Retrieve DAN 7:2-8 and DAN 7:17-24. Run cross-testament parallels on REV 13:1 and REV 13:2 (both OT and NT). Run greek_parser.py on REV 13:1-2 to examine exact vocabulary.

   WHY: The daniel-7-8-9-revelation-parallels study confirmed this is a verbatim Greek quotation. This verbal identity proves literary dependence and same-referent identification.
