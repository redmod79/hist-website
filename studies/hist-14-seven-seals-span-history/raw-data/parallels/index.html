    Joel 2:10 0.439 heaven, moon, star, sun
    Joel 3:4 0.425 moon, sun

    NT Parallels¶

    Reference Score Shared Terms
    MRK 13:24 0.709 after, darken, light, moon, sun, tribulation
    MRK 13:25 0.519 fall, heaven, power, shaken, star
    ACT 2:20 0.457 moon, sun
    REV 8:12 0.440 darken, moon, star, sun

    Key finding: Isa 13:10 is the strongest OT parallel to Mat 24:29 (score 0.490, 5 shared terms). Mat 24:29 follows Isaiah's wording more closely ("sun darkened, moon no light"). Rev 6:12 follows Joel 2:31 more closely ("moon as blood"). This vocabulary divergence suggests independent access to a shared OT prophetic tradition rather than direct literary dependence.

    NT Parallels¶

    Reference Score Shared Terms

    Psa 136:18 0.322 kill
    Neh 9:28 0.314 deliver
    Gen 34:25 0.305 kill
    Gen 37:20 0.301 kill

    NT Parallels¶

    Reference Score Shared Terms
    MAT 10:22 0.408 hat, name
    MRK 13:13 0.408 hat, name
    LUK 21:17 0.399 hat, name
    REV 11:18 0.377 name, nation
    LUK 21:12 0.351 name

    Key finding: The synoptic parallels (Mat 10:22, Mrk 13:13, Luk 21:17) all share the "hated for my name" formula. REV 11:18 surfaces as a parallel sharing "name" and "nation" — another connection between Olivet Discourse and Revelation. The tool does not strongly link to REV 6:9-11 because the fifth seal uses sphazo (G4969) for "slain" while Mat 24:9 uses apokteino (G615) for "kill" — different vocabulary for the same concept.
