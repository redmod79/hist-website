    Raw Strong's Lookup Data¶

    G4536 — σάλπιγξ (salpinx)¶

    • 11 total occurrences, 7 unique translations
    • Translations: of a trumpet (3), trumpets (2), trumpet (2), the trumpet (1), trump (1), the trump (1), a trumpet (1)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; perhaps from the idea of quavering or reverberation; a trumpet
    • Biblical Usage: Mat 24:31; 1Cor 14:8; 15:52; 1Th 4:16; Heb 12:19; Rev 1:10; 4:1; 8:2; 8:6; 8:13; 9:14

    G4537 — σαλπίζω (salpizo)¶

    • 13 total occurrences, 6 unique translations
    • Translations: sounded (7), to sound (2), do (1), sound a trumpet (1), the trumpet shall sound (1), sound (1)
    • 7 of the "sounded" occurrences form the esalpisen formula in Rev 8:7, 8:8, 8:10, 8:12, 9:1, 9:13, 11:15

    G3340 — μετανοέω (metanoeo)¶

    • 36 total occurrences, 21 unique translations
    • Translations: Repent/repent (8), they repented/repented (8), repent ye (3), plus 17 other forms
    • Key Revelation occurrences: 2:5, 2:16, 2:21, 2:22, 3:3, 3:19, 9:20, 9:21, 16:9, 16:11

    G987 — βλασφημέω (blasphemeo)¶

    • 36 total occurrences, 25 unique translations
    • Translations: blasphemed (6), to blaspheme (3), speak evil (3), railed (2), plus others
    • Key finding: 3x in bowls (16:9, 11, 21), ZERO in trumpets (9:20-21)

    G3709 — ὀργή (orge)¶

    • 33 total occurrences
    • Translations: wrath (21), the wrath (3), anger (3), of wrath (3), vengeance (1), unto wrath (1), indignation (1)
    • Revelation: 6:16, 6:17, 11:18, 14:10, 16:19, 19:15

    G2372 — θυμός (thymos)¶

    • 18 total occurrences
    • Translations: wrath (12), fierceness (2), with wrath (1), of wrath (1), indignation (1), wraths (1)
    • Revelation: 12:12, 14:8, 14:10, 14:19, 15:1, 15:7, 16:1, 16:19, 18:3, 19:15

    G2078 — ἔσχατος (eschatos)¶

    • 43 total occurrences, 10 unique translations
    • Translations: last (29), the last (5), lowest (2), plus others
    • Key: Rev 15:1 "the seven LAST (eschatas) plagues"

    G3031 — λιβανωτός (libanotos)¶

    • 2 total occurrences (ONLY 2 in entire NT)
    • Translations: censer (2)
    • Occurrences: Rev 8:3, Rev 8:5 — ONLY in the incense scene

    G2368 — θυμίαμα (thymiama)¶

    • 6 total occurrences
    • Translations: of incense (2), incense (2), of odours (1), odours (1)
    • Occurrences: Luke 1:10, 1:11; Rev 5:8, 8:3, 18:13 (2x)

    G2379 — θυσιαστήριον (thysiastērion)¶

    • 22 total occurrences
    • Translations: altar (20), altars (1), an altar (1)
    • Revelation occurrences: 6:9, 8:3 (2x), 8:5, 9:13, 11:1, 14:18, 16:7

    G5154 — τρίτος (tritos)¶

    • 53 total occurrences
    • Translations: third (27), third part (14), the third (6), the third time (3), plus others
    • Massive concentration in Rev 8-9 (13+ times), ZERO in Rev 16

    G3341 — μετάνοια (metanoia)¶

    • 24 total occurrences
    • Translations: repentance (14), of repentance (7), for repentance (2), that repentance (1)
    • Key: 2 Pet 3:9 "that all should come to repentance"

    H8643 — תְּרוּעָה (teruah)¶

    • 43 occurrences, 25 unique translations
    • Translations: shout (6), with shouting (5), with a (5), an alarm (4), of blowing of trumpets (1), of the jubile (1), plus others
    • Definition: clamor; acclamation of joy OR battle-cry; clangor of trumpets as an alarm
    • 36 total in BLB count

    H7782 — שׁוֹפָר (shophar)¶

    • 73 occurrences, 18 unique translations
    • Translations: of the trumpet (19), the trumpet (16), with the trumpets (8), a trumpet (8), the trumpets (6), trumpets (4), plus others
    • Definition: a cornet or curved horn giving a clear sound; 72 in BLB

    H3725 — כִּפֻּר (kippur)¶

    • 8 total occurrences
    • Translations: of atonement (4), atonement (1), of atonements (1), of the atonement (1), the (1)
    • Occurrences: Exo 29:36; 30:10; 30:16; Lev 23:27; 23:28; 25:9; Num 5:8; 29:11
    • Definition: expiation; only in plural (kippurim)
