    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    Do Revelation 1:7, 6:14-17, 14:14-20, and 19:11-21 describe the same event — the second coming of Christ? How do the winepress, cosmic signs, and universal visibility chains connect these passages? And is there biblical support for a separate "secret rapture" before the second coming?

    Topics¶

    RESURRECTION¶

    • General scriptures concerning: JOB 14:12-15; 19:25-27; PSA 16:9,10; 17:15; 49:15; ISA 25:8; 26:19; EZK 37:1-14; DAN 12:2,3,13; HOS 13:14; MAT 22:23-32; 24:31; 25:1-13; 27:52,53; MRK 12:18-27; LUK 14:14; 20:27-38; JHN 5:21,25,28,29; 6:39,40,44,54; 11:23-25; 14:19; ACT 2:26-31; 4:1,2; 17:18,32; 23:6,8; 24:14,15; 26:6-8; ROM 4:16-21; 8:10,11,19,21-23; 1CO 6:14; 15:12-32,35-57; 2CO 4:14; 5:1-5; PHP 3:10,11,21; 1TH 4:14,16; 2TI 1:10; 2:18; HEB 6:2; 11:19,35; REV 1:18; 20:4-6,13
    • FIGURATIVE: Of regeneration ROM 6:4; EPH 2:1,5,6; COL 2:12; 3:1
    • SYMBOLICAL: REV 11:11

    TRUMPET¶

    • Made of ram's horn JOS 6:4-6,8,13
    • Made of silver NUM 10:2
    • Uses prescribed by Moses NUM 10:1-10
    • Used in war JOB 39:24,25; JER 4:19; 6:1,17; 42:14; 51:27; EZK 7:14; AMO 2:2; 3:6; ZEP 1:16; 1CO 14:8
    • FIGURATIVE: ISA 27:13; EZK 33:3; JOL 2:1; ZEC 9:14; MAT 6:2
    • SYMBOLICAL: MAT 24:31; 1CO 15:52; 1TH 4:16; REV 1:10; 4:1; 8; 9:1-14; 10:7; 11:15

    JUDGMENT (THE GENERAL)¶

    1CH 16:33; JOB 14:17; 21:30; 31:13-15; PSA 9:7; 50:3-6; 96:13; 98:9; ECC 3:17; 11:9; 12:14; EZK 18:20-28; DAN 7:9,10; AMO 4:12; MAT 3:12; 7:22,23; 8:29; 10:15; 11:22; 12:36,37,41,42; 13:30,40-43,49,50; 16:27; 22:11-13; 23:14; 25:1-46; MRK 4:22; 8:38; 13:32; LUK 3:17; 10:10-14; 11:31,32; 12:2-5; 13:24-29; 19:12-26; 20:45-47; JHN 5:22; 12:48; ACT 2:19-21; 10:42; 17:31; 24:25; ROM 2:5-10,12-16; 14:10-12; 1CO 3:13; 4:5; 6:2; 2CO 5:10; 2TH 1:7,8; 2TI 4:1,8; HEB 6:2; 9:27; 10:27; 1PE 4:5,7; 2PE 2:4,9; 3:7,10-12; 1JN 4:17; Jude 1:6,14,15,24; REV 1:7; 6:15-17; 11:18; 20:11-15; 22:12

    WINE (Winepress references)¶

    • FIGURATIVE: Of divine judgments PSA 60:3; 75:8; JER 51:7
    • Of abominations REV 14:8; 16:19

    Winepress references from Nave's VINEYARD: - Winepress in ISA 5:2 - Towers in ISA 5:2; MAT 21:33; MRK 12:1

    From full-text search for "winepress": - ISA 63:2,3 — treading the winepress (figurative: judgment) - LAM 1:15 — "the Lord hath trodden the virgin, the daughter of Judah, as in a winepress" - REV 14:19,20 — "the great winepress of the wrath of God" - JOL 3:13 — "the press is full, the fats overflow; for their wickedness is great"

    EARTHQUAKES¶

    • General scriptures: JOB 9:6; PSA 18:7; 46:2,3; 104:32; JER 4:24
    • As judgments: PSA 18:15; 60:2; ISA 13:13,14; 24:19,20; 29:6; NAM 1:5
    • Prophecies of: EZK 38:19; ZEC 14:4; MAT 24:7; MRK 13:8; LUK 21:11; REV 11:19
    • SYMBOLICAL: REV 6:12-14; 11:13; 16:18,20

    CLOUD¶

    • FIGURATIVE: JER 4:13; HOS 6:4; 13:3
    • SYMBOLICAL: REV 14:14
    • Pillar of: EXO 13:21,22; 14:19,24; 16:10; 19:9,16; etc.

    LAMB OF GOD¶

    An name of Jesus: JHN 1:29; REV 6:16; 7:9,10,14,17; 12:11; 13:8; 14:1,4; 15:3; 17:14; 19:17; 21:9,14,22,23,27; 22:1,3

    ANTICHRIST¶

    • General scriptures: MAT 24:5,23,24,26; MRK 13:6,21,22; LUK 21:8; 2TH 2:3-12; 1JN 2:18,22; 4:3; 2JN 1:7
    • To be destroyed: REV 19:20; 20:10,15

    SIGN¶

    • A miracle to confirm faith: MAT 12:38; 16:4; 24:3,30; MRK 8:11,12; 13:4; JHN 2:11; 3:2; 4:48
    • A token of coming events: MAT 16:3,4; 24:3

    WRATH (G3709 orgē)¶

    Cross-references to ANGER; GOD, ANGER OF; JUDGMENT; PUNISHMENT, DIVINE Key Revelation references: REV 6:16 ("wrath of the Lamb"), REV 6:17 ("great day of his wrath"), REV 11:18, REV 14:10 ("wine of the wrath of God"), REV 16:19 ("cup of the wine of the fierceness of his wrath"), REV 19:15 ("winepress of the fierceness and wrath of Almighty God")

    Related Topics Discovered¶

    Joel 3:13 — The Combined Sickle + Winepress Source¶

    Joel 3:13 is the single OT verse combining both harvest images: "Put ye in the sickle, for the harvest is ripe: come, get you down; for the press is full." This is the source text for both Rev 14:15 (sickle) and 14:19 (winepress).

    Zechariah 14:1-5 — The LORD Coming with Saints¶

    Zech 14:5: "the LORD my God shall come, and all the saints with thee" — combines the Lord's physical coming with cosmic upheaval (Mount of Olives cleaving). Connected to Acts 1:11 (ascension from Olivet).
