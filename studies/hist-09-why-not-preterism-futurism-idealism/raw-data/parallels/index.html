    Raw Cross-Testament Parallels Data¶

    REV 12:5 (Christ's birth/ascension)¶

    OT Parallels¶

    Reference Hybrid Score Key Terms
    Micah 4:13 0.363 iron
    Nahum 3:10 0.329 child
    1 Chr 26:6 0.326 dominion
    Isa 9:5 0.319 dominion
    1 Ki 11:13 0.314 dominion, rod

    NT Parallels¶

    Reference Hybrid Score Key Terms
    REV 19:15 0.441 forth, iron, nation, rod
    REV 12:4 0.394 about, child, forth
    MAT 2:13 0.352 about, child
    REV 12:13 0.344 child, forth
    COL 1:13 0.338 dominion
    REV 2:27 0.333 iron, rod
    MAT 1:18 0.328 about, child

    NOTE: REV 19:15 is the highest NT match — Christ "shall rule them with a rod of iron," matching Rev 12:5's "rule all nations with a rod of iron." REV 2:27 also parallels with "rod of iron." These connect Christ's birth (12:5) with His second-coming judgment (19:15).

    Psa 90:12 0.333 count, wisdom
    Dan 1:20 0.327 understand, wisdom

    NT Parallels¶

    Reference Hybrid Score Key Terms
    LUK 15:4 0.361 having, hundred
    REV 13:17 0.353 beast, having, number
    JAS 3:13 0.343 understand, wisdom
    REV 17:9 0.338 having, wisdom
    REV 17:7 0.315 beast, having

    NOTE: REV 17:9 parallels with "wisdom" — "And here is the mind which hath wisdom" echoes "Here is wisdom" in 13:18. Both call for wisdom/understanding to decode symbols.
