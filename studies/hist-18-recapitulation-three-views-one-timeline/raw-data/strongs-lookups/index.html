    G3709 — orge (wrath)¶

    • Transliteration: orge
    • Pronunciation: or-gay
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 36
    • Definition: properly, desire (as a reaching forth or excitement of the mind); by analogy, violent passion; by implication, punishment — anger, indignation, vengeance, wrath

    All Translations (33 total occurrences in KJV concordance)¶

    Translation Count %
    wrath 21 63.6%
    the wrath 3 9.1%
    anger 3 9.1%
    of wrath 3 9.1%
    vengeance 1 3.0%
    unto wrath 1 3.0%
    indignation 1 3.0%

    Verse List for "wrath" Translation¶

    Luk 21:23, Rom 2:5, Rom 2:8, Rom 4:15, Rom 5:9, Rom 9:22, Rom 13:4, Rom 13:5, Eph 5:6, Col 3:6, 1Th 1:10, 1Th 2:16, 1Th 5:9, 1Ti 2:8, Heb 3:11, Heb 4:3, Rev 6:16, Rev 6:17, Rev 11:18, Rev 16:19, Rev 19:15

    Full Biblical Usage¶

    G2372 — thymos (fury/wrath)¶

    • Transliteration: thymos
    • Pronunciation: thoo-mos
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 18
    • Definition: from thyo; passion (as if breathing hard) — fierceness, indignation, wrath

    All Translations (18 total)¶

    Translation Count %
    wrath 12 66.7%
    fierceness 2 11.1%
    with wrath 1 5.6%
    of wrath 1 5.6%
    indignation 1 5.6%
    wraths 1 5.6%

    "Fierceness" Translation (2 verses)¶

    Rev 16:19, Rev 19:15

    "Wrath" Translation (12 verses)¶

    Full Biblical Usage¶

    G2064 — erchomai (come)¶

    • Transliteration: erchomai
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 643
    • Definition: middle voice of a primary verb; to come or go
    • 482 total occurrences in KJV, 115 unique translations
    • Top translations: came (67), come (54), cometh (30), to come (21)

    Key Revelation Occurrences¶

    Rev 1:4, 2:5, 2:16, 4:8, 5:7, 6:1, 6:3 (2x), 6:5, 6:7, 6:17 (elthen), 7:13, 7:14, 14:7 (elthen), 14:15, 17:1, 17:10, 18:10, 19:7, 21:9, 22:7

    G1096 — ginomai (become/happen)¶

    • Transliteration: ginomai
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 678
    • Definition: to cause to be ("gen"-erate); to become, come into being
    • 629 total occurrences, 218 unique translations

    Key Revelation Occurrences¶

    Rev 1:10, 4:2, 6:12, 8:5, 8:11, 11:13, 11:15 (2x), 12:7, 12:10, 16:2 (2x), 16:17 (Gegonen), 16:18, 16:19, 21:6 (Gegonen)

    "It is done" Occurrences (Gegonen — Perfect)¶

    Rev 16:17 — "Gegonen" = "It is done" (7th bowl) Rev 21:6 — "Gegonen" = "It is done" (new creation)

    G5154 — tritos (third)¶

    • Transliteration: tritos
    • Part of Speech: adjective (adj)
    • BLB Count: 57
    • Definition: ordinal from treis; third; neuter as noun: a third part
    • 53 total occurrences, 7 unique translations

    "Third Part" Translations (14 verses — ALL in Revelation trumpets except 12:4)¶

    Rev 8:7, 8:8, 8:9 (2x), 8:10 (2x), 8:11, 8:12 (5x), 9:15, 9:18, 12:4

    "Third" Translations (in Revelation — non-fraction uses)¶

    Rev 4:7 (third beast), 6:5 (third seal), 11:14 (third woe)

    G2078 — eschatos (last)¶

    • Transliteration: eschatos
    • Part of Speech: adjective (superlative)
    • BLB Count: 54
    • 43 total occurrences, 10 unique translations
    • Top: "last" (29), "the last" (5), "lowest" (2)

    Key Revelation Occurrences¶

    Rev 1:11, 1:17, 2:8, 2:19, 15:1 (seven last plagues), 21:9 (seven last plagues), 22:13

    G3528 — nikao (overcome/conquer)¶

    • Transliteration: nikao
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 28
    • Definition: from nike; to subdue — conquer, overcome, prevail, get the victory
    • 22 total occurrences, 13 unique translations

    All Revelation Occurrences (17 verses)¶

    G4536 — salpinx (trumpet, instrument)¶

    • Part of Speech: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 11
    • Revelation occurrences: 1:10, 4:1, 8:2, 8:6, 8:13, 9:14

    G4537 — salpizo (to trumpet, verb)¶

    • Part of Speech: verb
    • BLB Count: 12
    • Revelation occurrences: 8:6, 8:7, 8:8, 8:10, 8:12, 9:1, 9:13, 10:7, 11:15
    • "sounded" = 7 times (all 7 trumpet blasts)

    G3520 — nesos (island)¶

    • Part of Speech: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 9
    • Definition: an island
    • Literal: Acts 13:6, 27:26, 28:1, 28:7, 28:9, 28:11, Rev 1:9
    • Prophetic/symbolic: Rev 6:14, 16:20 ONLY

    G2795 — kineo (move/remove)¶

    • Part of Speech: verb
    • BLB Count: 8
    • Definition: to stir, move
    • Revelation: 2:5 ("remove thy candlestick"), 6:14 ("were moved")
    • Other: Mat 23:4, 27:39, Mk 15:29, Acts 17:28, 21:30, 24:5

    G5343 — pheugo (flee)¶

    • Part of Speech: verb
    • BLB Count: 31
    • Definition: to run away, flee; by implication, to vanish
    • Revelation: 9:6, 12:6, 16:20 ("fled away"), 20:11 ("fled away")
    • Common: Mat 2:13, 3:7, 24:16, Mk 13:14, etc.

    G3710 — orgizo (to be wroth/angry)¶

    • Part of Speech: verb
    • BLB Count: 8
    • Definition: from orge; to provoke or enrage; passively: become exasperated
    • Revelation: 11:18 ("were angry" — orgisthesan), 12:17 ("was wroth")
    • Other: Mat 5:22, 18:34, 22:7, Lk 14:21, 15:28, Eph 4:26

    G5456 — phone (voice/sound)¶

    • Part of Speech: feminine noun
    • BLB Count: 141
    • 126 total in KJV
    • Top: voice (62), a voice (18), the voice (17), voices (14), sound (4)
    • Revelation alone: ~55 occurrences (most frequent in Revelation)
    • Key structural uses: 4:5, 8:5, 11:15, 11:19, 16:17, 16:18

    G5067 — tetartos (fourth)¶

    • Part of Speech: adjective
    • BLB Count: 10
    • Definition: ordinal from tessares; fourth
    • Key: Rev 6:8 — "fourth part [tetarton] of the earth"
    • Also: Rev 4:7 (fourth beast), Rev 8:12 (fourth angel sounded)
