    TRUMPETS (Feast of)¶

    • When and how observed: LEV 23:24,25; NUM 29:1-6
    • Celebrated after the captivity with joy: NEH 8:2,9-12
    • See FEASTS

    TRUMPET¶

    • Made of ram's horn: JOS 6:4-6,8,13
    • Made of silver: NUM 10:2
    • Uses prescribed by Moses: NUM 10:1-10
    • Used in war: JOB 39:24,25; JER 4:19; 6:1,17; 42:14; 51:27; EZK 7:14; AMO 2:2; 3:6; ZEP 1:16; 1CO 14:8
    • To summon soldiers: Phinehas (NUM 31:6), Ehud (JDG 3:27), Gideon (JDG 6:34), Saul (1SA 13:3), Joab (2SA 2:28; 18:16; 20:22), Absalom (2SA 15:10), Sheba (2SA 20:1), Nehemiah (NEH 4:18,20)
    • Gideon's soldiers: JDG 7:8-22
    • War of Abijah: 2CH 13:12,14
    • Siege of Jericho: JOS 6:4-20
    • Time of danger: EZK 33:3-6; JOL 2:1
    • Mount Sinai: EXO 19:13-19; 20:18; HEB 12:19
    • Day of atonement: ISA 27:13
    • Jubilee: LEV 25:9
    • Bringing up ark: 2SA 6:5,15; 1CH 13:8; 15:28
    • Anointing kings: 1KI 1:34,39; 2KI 9:13; 11:14
    • Temple dedication: 2CH 5:12,13; 7:6
    • Worship: 1CH 15:24; 16:42; 25:5; PSA 81:3,4
    • Jehoshaphat triumph: 2CH 20:28
    • Foundation second temple: EZR 3:10,11
    • Wall dedication: NEH 12:35,41
    • FIGURATIVE: ISA 27:13; EZK 33:3; JOL 2:1; ZEC 9:14; MAT 6:2
    • SYMBOLICAL: MAT 24:31; 1CO 15:52; 1TH 4:16; REV 1:10; 4:1; 8; 9:1-14; 10:7; 11:15

    WRATH¶

    (Anger, judgment, punishment) Cross-references: ANGER, GOD ANGER OF, JUDGMENT, PUNISHMENT DIVINE, PUNISHMENT ETERNAL

    ANGER OF GOD (from ANGER entry)¶

    EXO 22:24; 32:10; 33:5; NUM 11:1,10,33; 12:9; 14:11; 16:20,21,45; 25:3,4,11; 32:10,11,13; DEU 6:14,15; 9:13,14,18-20; 32:21,22; JOS 7:1,26; 23:16; JDG 2:12; 3:8; 10:7; 1SA 28:18; 2SA 6:7; 22:8,9; 1KI 11:9; 16:2-13; 2KI 13:3; 17:18; 22:13; 23:26; PSA 7:11; 69:24; 74:1; 76:7; 78:21,38,49,50; 85:3; 90:11; 103:8; 106:23,29,32; 110:5; ISA 5:25; 9:17,19,21; 12:1; 13:9,13; 30:27; 42:25; 48:9; 57:16,17; 63:3-6; 66:15; JER 3:12; 4:4,8,26; 7:20; 10:10; 17:4; 21:5,6; 23:20; 25:15-17,37,38; 30:24; 32:37; 33:5; 36:7; 42:18; 44:6; 51:45; LAM 2:1,3,6; 4:11; EZK 5:13,15; 25:14-17; DAN 9:16; HOS 11:9; 13:11; 14:4; NAM 1:2,3,6; MAT 22:7,13; ROM 1:18; 2:5; EPH 5:6; COL 3:6; HEB 3:11; 4:3; REV 6:16,17; 14:10,11; 15:1,7; 16:19; 19:15

    EARTHQUAKES¶

    • General: JOB 9:6; PSA 18:7; 46:2,3; 104:32; JER 4:24
    • Judgments: PSA 18:15; 60:2; ISA 13:13,14; 24:19,20; 29:6; NAM 1:5
    • Prophecies: EZK 38:19; ZEC 14:4; MAT 24:7; MRK 13:8; LUK 21:11; REV 11:19
    • Instances: Sinai (EXO 19:18; PSA 68:8; 77:18; 114:4-7; HEB 12:26), Korah (NUM 16:31,32), Jonathan (1SA 14:15), Elijah (1KI 19:11), Uzziah (AMO 1:1; ZEC 14:5), Crucifixion (MAT 27:51), Resurrection (MAT 28:2), Paul/Silas (ACT 16:26)
    • FIGURATIVE: PSA 60:2
    • SYMBOLICAL: REV 6:12-14; 11:13; 16:18,20

    MOUNTAIN¶

    • Melted: PSA 97:5; DEU 4:11; 5:23; JDG 5:5; ISA 64:1-3; MIC 1:4; NAM 1:5
    • Overturning/removing: JOB 9:5; 14:18; 28:9; EZK 38:20
    • Removed by faith: MAT 17:20; 21:21; MRK 11:23

    ISLAND¶

    • Prophecies: PSA 97:1; ISA 11:11; 41:1,5; 49:1; ZEP 2:11; REV 16:20

    SEAL¶

    • Stamp for documents: GEN 38:18
    • Engraved: EXO 28:11,21,36; 39:6,14,30; 2TI 2:19
    • Decrees: 1KI 21:8; EST 8:8
    • FIGURATIVE — Secrecy: DAN 12:9; REV 5:1
    • Certainty/approval: JHN 6:27; ROM 4:11; 15:28; 2CO 1:22; EPH 1:13; 4:30; REV 7:3,4

    ARMAGEDDON¶

    • A symbolical name: REV 16:16

    SIGN¶

    • Miracle to confirm faith: MAT 12:38; 16:4; 24:3,30; MRK 8:11,12; 13:4; JHN 2:11; 3:2; 4:48
    • Token of coming events: MAT 16:3,4; 24:3

    REVELATION¶

    • God reveals himself: EXO 3:1-6,14; 6:1-3
    • Law revealed: EXO 20-35; LEV 1-7
    • Temple pattern: 1CH 28:11-19
    • Sonship of Jesus: MAT 3:17; 16:17; 17:5

    PROPHECY¶

    • Inspired: ISA 28:22; LUK 1:70; 2TI 3:16; 2PE 1:21
    • Sure fulfillment: EZK 12:22-25,28; HAB 2:3; MAT 5:18; 24:35
    • Tribulations of righteous: REV 2:10
