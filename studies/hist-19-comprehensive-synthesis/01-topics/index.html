    Study Clusters¶

    CLUSTER 1: FOUNDATION (Studies 01-06)¶

    These studies establish the hermeneutical framework and the Daniel backbone.

    hist-01: How to Read Apocalyptic Prophecy Establishes: semaino/propheteia genre markers, angel-interpreter principle, four-kingdom succession, day-year formula texts, Daniel-Revelation literary connection, sealed/unsealed arc. Key contribution: 47 E-items founding the entire framework. Two I-B Anti-Historicist claims resolved (en tachei, Antiochus).

    hist-02: Daniel 7 -- Beasts, Little Horn, Judgment Establishes: Four-beast sequence, ten-horn division, little horn specifications, time-times-half-a-time, judgment scene (Dan 7:9-14), Son of Man approaching God (not earth). Key contribution: Identification of the fourth kingdom and little horn; judgment mechanism linking to Dan 8:14. I-B resolved Strong (Greece-as-fourth-kingdom).

    hist-03: The 70 Weeks -- Jesus Fulfills the Timeline Establishes: Day-year principle empirically validated, 457 BC starting point, continuous 70 weeks (no gap), Messianic subject of Dan 9:27. Key contribution: The single strongest empirical proof of the day-year principle -- 483 years from 457 BC to AD 27 (Jesus' baptism).

    hist-04: Daniel 8 -- The Little Horn Identified Establishes: Gadal/yether progression, az panim cross-reference to Deut 28:50, mehem grammar, "time of the end" scope, "broken without hand" divine termination. Key contribution: Rome as Daniel 8's little horn via convergence of textual constraints. Two I-B resolved Strong (Antiochus identification fails).

    hist-05: Daniel 8-9 Connected -- The 2300 Days Establishes: Gabriel's return mission, mar'eh/chazon distinction, chathak = "cut off," 70 weeks cut from 2300 days, 2300-year terminus at 1844. Key contribution: The organic connection between Daniel 8 and 9, establishing the 2300-day scope and terminus. Three I-B resolved (all against Anti-Historicist).

    hist-06: The Sanctuary Vindicated -- 1844 Establishes: Nitsdaq = forensic vindication (not ritual cleansing), LXX anomaly in Dan 8:14, evening-morning = creation pattern (not Day of Atonement formula), Dan 7:9-10 / Dan 8:14 connection. Key contribution: What begins in 1844 -- a forensic judgment process, not a ritual cleansing. I-B resolved Strong (LXX override fails).

    CLUSTER 2: SEQUENCES (Studies 07-17)¶

    These studies trace Revelation's major prophetic sequences through history.

    hist-07: NT Connects Daniel 7-12 Establishes: Rev 1:1 echoes Dan 2:28 LXX, sealed-to-unsealed arc, composite beast absorbs four kingdoms, Paul's man of sin parallels Daniel, "already at work" / "already in the world" markers. Key contribution: The Daniel-Revelation connection as a systematic literary and theological framework.

    hist-08: "Shortly Come to Pass" Establishes: En tachei semantic range (manner + temporal), hypomonE co-occurrence pattern, "little season" temporal extensions, inaugurated eschatology reading. Key contribution: En tachei resolved as inaugurated eschatology -- the beginning of the end, not the end itself.

    hist-09: Why Not Preterism, Futurism, Idealism? Establishes: Each alternative fails on specific textual data. Preterism cannot compress past-to-future span. Futurism requires gap and Israel/Church distinction demolished by six NT texts. Idealism cannot account for named empires and precise time periods. Key contribution: Systematic demonstration that only historicism accommodates ALL textual constraints. Five I-D items registered against alternatives.

    hist-10: The Seven Churches Establishes: Three-layer reading (literal, prophetic, universal), "till I come" anchor, progressive Second Coming language intensification, overcomer promises project to Rev 19-22, ekklesia inclusio. Key contribution: Textual proof that the churches extend to the Second Coming. I-B resolved Strong (exclusively-literal reading fails).

    hist-11: Revelation 12 -- Woman, Dragon, 1260 Establishes: Man child = Christ (Psa 2:9 allusion), 1260 days = Dan 7:25 verbatim LXX, seven cross-references to same duration, post-1260 remnant defined by commandments + testimony. Key contribution: The inescapable historical timeline: past anchor (12:5) -> extended period (12:6,14) -> present-era remnant (12:17).

    hist-12: Revelation 13-14 -- Beast and Three Angels Establishes: Composite beast absorbs all four Daniel beasts, verbatim Greek of Dan 7:8, 42 months = Dan 7:25, three angels positioned between beast authority and harvest, harvest = end of the world (Matt 13:39). Key contribution: Rev 12-14 as a continuous timeline from apostolic era to second coming. Three I-B resolved.

    hist-13: The Olivet Discourse Spans History Establishes: Dual-question structure (temple + parousia), tote chain markers, "the end is not yet," worldwide gospel prerequisite, "times of the Gentiles," near/far demonstrative pivot. Key contribution: The Olivet Discourse as a historical template spanning the apostolic era to the Second Coming.

    hist-14: The Seven Seals Span History Establishes: Nikao chain (Rev 5:5 -> 6:2), stephanos crown type, leukos symbolism, fifth seal's three temporal phases, sixth seal cosmic signs matching Matt 24:29. Key contribution: The fifth seal as the strongest internal proof of extended duration in Revelation.

    hist-15: Trumpets -- Warnings Before Judgment Establishes: Incense/intercession active (Rev 8:3-4), 1/3 limitation, "repented not" presupposes open probation, temple closed at bowls (Rev 15:8), Feast of Trumpets precedes Day of Atonement. Key contribution: Trumpets as warnings during intercession -- a distinct pre-judgment phase.

    hist-16: Bowls -- After Judgment Establishes: "Seven last plagues" (eschatai), no temple entry (Rev 15:8), zero fraction words, passive non-repentance -> active blasphemy escalation, "It is done" (Gegonen). Key contribution: Bowls as post-probation judgment, completing the warning-to-judgment trajectory.

    hist-17: One Second Coming, Many Angles Establishes: Winepress chain, wrath chain, cosmic destruction chain, universal visibility, apantesis civic delegation term, three coming-words interchangeable, no secret rapture. Key contribution: All Revelation "coming" passages describe one event. Three I-B resolved Strong (secret rapture, AD 70 fulfillment, Rev 4:1 rapture all fail).

    CLUSTER 3: SYNTHESIS (Study 18)¶

    Brings the sequences together.

    hist-18: Recapitulation -- Three Views, One Timeline Establishes: Identical elthen he orge endpoint formula, chronological regression at Rev 12:1-5, fraction escalation (1/4 -> 1/3 -> total), theophany formula accumulation, nikao chain spanning all sections, trumpet-bowl domain correspondence, intercession contrast. Key contribution: Structural proof that Revelation's sequences recapitulate the same historical span from different perspectives.
