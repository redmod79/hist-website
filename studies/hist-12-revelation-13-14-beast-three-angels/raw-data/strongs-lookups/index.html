    Raw Strong's Lookup Data¶

    G2342 — θηρίον (therion) — beast¶

    • Lookup: 45 occurrences, 6 translations: beast (36, 80%), wild beasts (3), beasts (2), a beast (2), The beast (1), the beast (1)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; diminutive of thēr; "a dangerous animal: (venomous, wild) beast"
    • BLB Count: 46
    • Usage: Mark 1:13; Acts 10:12; 11:6; 28:4; Titus 1:12; Heb 12:20; Jas 3:7; Rev 6:8; 11:7; 13; 14; 15; 16; 17; 18; 19; 20
    • Verses (beast, 36x): Act 28:4,5; Rev 11:7; 13:2,3,4(×3),11,12(×2),14(×2),15(×3),17,18; 14:9,11; 15:2; 16:2,10,13; 17:3,7,8,11,12,13,16,17; 19:19,20(×2); 20:4

    G2768 — κέρας (keras) — horn¶

    • Lookup: 11 occurrences, 2 translations: horns (10, 91%), an horn (1)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; from kar (hair of the head); "a horn (literally or figuratively)"
    • BLB Count: 11
    • Usage: Exo 29:12; Lev 4:7,18; 16:18; 1 Sam 2:10; 2 Sam 22:3; Psa 17:3; 18:3; 88:18; 89:18; 118:27; 132:17; 148:14; Amos 3:14; Luke 1:69; Rev 5:6; 9:13; 12:3; 13:1,11; 17:3,7,12,16

    G4750 — στόμα (stoma) — mouth¶

    • Lookup: 72 occurrences, 8 translations: mouth (53, 74%), the mouth (11), a mouth (2), mouths (2), by the edge (1), with the mouth (1), the mouths (1), the edge (1)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; "the mouth (as if a gash in the face)"
    • BLB Count: 79
    • Usage includes: Gen 34:26; Num 12:8; Deut 8:3; 19:15; Psa 8:3; 32:6; 33:6; Isa 11:4; 29:13; 45:23; 53:9; Matt 4:4; 12:34; 15:8; Luke 1:70; Acts 1:16; 3:18; Rom 3:19; 10:8,10; Eph 6:19; 2 Thess 2:8; Rev 1:16; 2:16; 3:16; 9:17; 12:15,16; 13:2,5; 14:5

    G2098 — εὐαγγέλιον (euangelion) — gospel¶

    • Lookup: 68 occurrences, 3 translations: gospel (64, 94%), gospel's (3), the gospel (1)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; from euangelos; "a good message, i.e. the gospel"
    • BLB Count: 77
    • Usage includes: Matt 4:23; 9:35; 24:14; 26:13; Mark 1:1,14,15; 8:35; 10:29; 13:10; 14:9; 16:15; Acts 15:7; 20:24; Rom 1:1,16; 1 Cor 9:12,14,18,23; 15:1; 2 Cor 4:3,4; Gal 1:6,7,11; 2:2,5,7,14; Eph 1:13; 3:6; 6:15,19; Phil 1:5,27; Col 1:5,23; 1 Thess 1:5; 2:2,4; 2 Thess 1:8; 2:14; 1 Tim 1:11; 2 Tim 1:8,10; 2:8; Rev 14:6

    G166 — αἰώνιος (aionios) — eternal/everlasting¶

    • Lookup: 48 occurrences, 12 translations: eternal (19, 40%), everlasting (13, 27%), of eternal (7), plus 9 minor variants
    • Lexicon: adjective; from aion; "perpetual (also used of past time, or past and future)"
    • BLB Count: 71
    • Usage includes: Num 25:13; Eccl 12:5; Matt 25:41,46; Mark 3:29; Luke 16:9; John 14:2; Rom 16:25,26; 2 Cor 4:17,18; 2 Thess 1:9; 2:16; 2 Tim 1:9; 2:10; Titus 1:2; Heb 5:9; 6:2; 9:12,14,15; 1 Pet 5:10; 2 Pet 1:11; Rev 14:6
    • NOTE: Rev 14:6 "everlasting gospel" (εὐαγγέλιον αἰώνιον) is the ONLY occurrence of this exact phrase in the NT

    G2920 — κρίσις (krisis) — judgment¶

    • Lookup: 34 occurrences, 6 translations: judgment (18, 53%), of judgment (8), damnation (2), the judgment (2), accusation (2), judgments (2)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; "decision (subjectively or objectively, for or against)"
    • BLB Count: 48
    • Usage includes: Gen 18:19,25; Deut 16:18; Isa 5:7; 53:8; 56:1; 59:8; Dan 7:10,26; Matt 5:21; 10:15; 11:22,24; 12:18,20,36,41; 23:23,33; John 3:19; 5:24,27,29,30; 7:24; 12:31; 16:8,11; Acts 8:33; 2 Thess 1:5; Heb 9:27; 10:27; Jas 2:13; 2 Pet 2:4,9; 3:7; 1 John 4:17; Jude 1:6,9,15; Rev 14:7; 16:7; 18:10; 19:2
    • Verses (judgment, 18x): Matt 5:21,22; 12:18,20,41,42; 23:23; Luke 10:14; 11:31,32,42; Acts 8:33; 2 Thess 1:5; 1 Tim 5:24; 2 Pet 2:4; Jude 1:15; Rev 14:7; 18:10

    G4352 — προσκυνέω (proskuneo) — worship¶

    • Lookup: 53 occurrences, 16 translations: worship (13, 25%), worshipped (12, 23%), and worshipped (7), to worship (4), plus 12 minor variants
    • Lexicon: verb; from pros + probable derivative of kuon (to kiss, like a dog licking); "to fawn or crouch to, i.e. prostrate oneself in homage"
    • BLB Count: 60
    • Usage includes: Matt 2:2,8,11; 4:9,10; John 4:20,21,23; Acts 7:43; 24:11; Rev 3:9; 4:10; 5:14; 7:11; 9:20; 11:1,16; 13:4(×2),12,15; 14:7,9,11; 15:4; 16:2; 19:4,10,20; 20:4; 22:8
    • Verses (worship, 13x): Heb 1:6; Rev 3:9; 4:10; 9:20; 11:1; 13:12,15; 14:7,9,11; 15:4; 19:10; 22:9

    G5480 — χάραγμα (charagma) — mark¶

    • Lookup: 9 occurrences, 3 translations: mark (7, 78%), graven (1), a mark (1)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; from charasso; "a scratch or etching, i.e. stamp (as a badge of servitude)"
    • BLB Count: 9
    • Usage: Acts 17:29; Rev 13:16; 14:9,11; 15:2; 16:2; 19:20; 20:4
    • Verses (mark, 7x): Rev 13:17; 14:9,11; 15:2; 16:2; 19:20; 20:4
    • NOTE: 8 of 9 total uses are in Revelation (13-20), exclusively in beast/worship contexts

    G1504 — εἰκών (eikon) — image¶

    • Lookup: 23 occurrences, 4 translations: image (17, 74%), the image (3), an image (2), the image of him (1)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; from eiko; "a likeness, i.e. (literally) statue, profile, or (figuratively) representation"
    • BLB Count: 23
    • Usage: Matt 22:20; Mark 12:16; Luke 20:24; Rom 1:23; 8:29; 1 Cor 11:7; 15:49; 2 Cor 3:18; 4:4; Phil 3:21; Col 1:15; 3:10; Heb 10:1; Rev 13:14(×2); 14:9,11; 15:2; 16:2; 19:20; 20:4
    • Verses (image, 17x): Matt 22:20; Mark 12:16; Luke 20:24; Rom 8:29; 1 Cor 15:49(×2); 2 Cor 3:18; Heb 10:1; Rev 13:15(×3); 14:9,11; 15:2; 16:2; 19:20; 20:4

    G5281 — ὑπομονή (hupomone) — patience¶

    • Lookup: 29 occurrences, 7 translations: patience (22, 76%), of patience (2), patient continuance (1), the enduring (1), the patient waiting (1), in patience (1), the patience (1)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; from hupomeno; "cheerful (or hopeful) endurance, constancy"
    • BLB Count: 32
    • Usage includes: Job 14:19; Psa 9:19; 38:8; 39:8; 62:6; 71:5; Prov 10:28; Jer 14:8; 17:13; Luke 8:15; 21:19; Rom 2:7; 5:3; 8:25; 15:4; 2 Cor 1:6; 6:4; 12:12; Col 1:11; 1 Thess 1:3; 2 Thess 1:4; 3:5; 1 Tim 6:11; 2 Tim 3:10; Titus 2:2; Heb 10:36; 12:1; Jas 1:3; 5:11; 2 Pet 1:6; Rev 1:9; 2:2,19; 3:10; 13:10; 14:12

    G4102 — πίστις (pistis) — faith¶

    • Lookup: 222 occurrences, 17 translations: faith (153, 69%), of faith (22), By faith (15), the faith (10), by faith (6), plus 12 minor variants
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; from peitho; "persuasion, i.e. credence; moral conviction (of religious truth)"
    • BLB Count: 244
    • Key Revelation uses: Rev 2:13,19; 13:10; 14:12
    • NOTE: Rev 14:12 "the faith of Jesus" (πίστιν Ἰησοῦ) — subjective genitive = faith that Jesus himself had; objective genitive = faith IN Jesus. Both readings theologically significant.

    G988 — βλασφημία (blasphemia) — blasphemy¶

    • Lookup: 18 occurrences, 7 translations: blasphemy (7, 39%), blasphemies (5, 28%), of blasphemy (2), the blasphemy (1), evil speaking (1), railings (1), a railing (1)
    • Lexicon: feminine noun; from blasphemos; "vilification (especially against God)"
    • BLB Count: 19
    • Usage: Ezek 35:12; Matt 12:31(×2); 15:19; 26:65; Mark 2:7; 3:28; 7:22; 14:64; Luke 5:21; John 10:33; Eph 4:31; Col 3:8; 1 Tim 6:4; 2 Pet 2:11; Jude 1:9; Rev 2:9; 13:1,5,6; 17:3
    • Verses (blasphemy, 7x): Matt 12:31; 26:65; Mark 7:22; 14:64; Col 3:8; Rev 2:9; 13:6

    G4098 — πίπτω (pipto) — fall¶

    • Lookup: 89 occurrences, 34 translations; "is fallen" (4x): Rev 14:8(×2); 18:2(×2)
    • Lexicon: verb; "to fall (literally or figuratively)"
    • BLB Count: 90
    • Key uses: Jer 51:8 (LXX); Rev 14:8; 18:2 — doubled aorist ἔπεσεν ἔπεσεν (epesen epesen)
    • Verses ("is fallen", 4x): Rev 14:8(×2); Rev 18:2(×2)
    • NOTE: The doubled ἔπεσεν ἔπεσεν in Rev 14:8 and 18:2 directly echoes Isa 21:9 LXX πέπτωκεν Βαβυλών (Babylon has fallen)

    G1407 — δρέπανον (drepanon) — sickle¶

    • Lookup: 8 occurrences, 1 translation: sickle (8, 100%)
    • Lexicon: neuter noun; from drepo (to pluck); "a gathering hook (especially for harvesting)"
    • BLB Count: 8
    • Usage: Mark 4:29; Rev 14:14,15,16,17,18,19
    • Verses (sickle, 8x): Mark 4:29; Rev 14:14,15,16,17,18(×2),19
    • NOTE: 7 of 8 NT uses are concentrated in Rev 14:14-19 harvest scene

    G2326 — θερισμός (therismos) — harvest¶

    • Lookup: 11 occurrences, 2 translations: harvest (10, 91%), of harvest (1)
    • Lexicon: masculine noun; from therizo; "reaping, i.e. the crop"
    • BLB Count: 13
    • Usage: Matt 9:37,38; 13:30,39; Mark 4:29; Luke 10:2; John 4:35; Rev 14:15
    • Verses (harvest, 10x): Matt 9:37,38(×2); 13:30,39; Mark 4:29; Luke 10:2(×3); Rev 14:15
    • NOTE: Jesus identifies "the harvest is the end of the world" in Matt 13:39 → Rev 14:15 harvest = eschatological judgment
