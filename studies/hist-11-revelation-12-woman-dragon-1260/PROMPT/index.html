    Bible Study: Revelation 12 — The Woman, the Dragon, and the 1260 Years¶

    From revelation-historicist-proof/12-rev12-woman-dragon-child.md: - Rev 12:5 is EXPLICITLY Christ: "rod of iron" (Ps 2:9) + "caught up unto God, and to his throne" (ascension). Evidence Grade: E (Explicit) - The 1260-day period appears in 7 references across Daniel and Revelation, all describing the same historical period - Historical fulfillment: 538 AD (Justinian's decree / Ostrogoths defeated) to 1798 AD (Berthier captures pope) - Rev 12:17 remnant is AFTER the 1260 years: "keep the commandments of God and have the testimony of Jesus" - Timeline spans ~2000 years: Christ's birth -> 1260 years (538-1798) -> remnant (post-1798 to Second Coming) - Preterism fails because 1260 years extends to 1798; futurism fails because Christ's birth (v.5) is already past

    From revelation-historicist-proof/15-rev12-14-structural-proof.md: - Rev 12-14 provides DEFINITIVE structural proof: starts with Christ's birth (12:5), ends with harvest/Second Coming (14:14-20) - Sequential events REQUIRE time: rise -> reign -> wound -> healing cannot be compressed into brief period - The Day of Atonement arc spans Rev 12-14: sacrifice -> intercession -> judgment announced -> final warning -> separation - Only historicism accounts for elements spanning past, history, and future

    From revelation-12-timing/CONCLUSION.md: - Rev 12 contains TWO distinct events: primordial rebellion (12:4a, before Christ's birth in narrative) and final expulsion at Christ's victory (12:7-12) - Jude 6 and 2 Pet 2:4 parallel verse 4a (primordial). Rev 12:7-12 is the later, final expulsion - Satan had heavenly access until Christ's victory: Job 1:6, Zech 3:1, Rev 12:10 ("accused them before our God day and night") - "NOW is come salvation... the power of his Christ" (12:10) and "by the blood of the Lamb" (12:11) tie the expulsion to the cross - John 12:31 confirms: "Now shall the prince of this world be cast out"

    From time-times-half-time/CONCLUSION.md: - Seven prophetic time expressions = same 3.5-year period (3.5 years x 360 = 1260 days = 42 months) - Aramaic 'iddan means "year" (proven by Dan 4's "seven times" = seven years) - Revelation 12:14 DIRECTLY QUOTES the LXX of Daniel 7:25 (kairon kai kairous kai hemisy kairou) - Day-year principle: consistent with Daniel 9's proven 70 weeks = 490 years - Historical fulfillment: 538 AD -> 1798 AD = 1260 years

    From revs-33-three-and-half-years/CONCLUSION.md: - Contextual differentiation pattern: 42 months always in hostile-activity contexts (trampling, blasphemy); 1260 days always in preservation/witness contexts (prophesying, feeding); time-times-half = Danielic cosmic formula - Divine passive (edothe) governs both persecution (Rev 13:5,7) and protection (Rev 12:14) — same divine Agent limits both - Rev 12:6 and 12:14 describe the SAME event with different vocabulary (active trephosin vs passive trephetai) - The 3.5 days of dead witnesses (Rev 11:9,11) echo the 3.5-year period in compressed form - OT allusion: Rev 12:14 "wings of eagle" echoes Exo 19:4 (divine rescue in wilderness)

    Historical Context from Web Research¶

    538 AD — Start of the 1260-Year Period: - Siege of Rome (537-538): Belisarius, commanding Justinian's Byzantine forces, defended Rome against the Ostrogoth army under King Vitiges. On March 12, 538, Vitiges abandoned the siege after 374 days. - Pope Vigilius ascended the papal chair under Belisarius's military protection in 538, after Pope Silverius was deposed. - Justinian had addressed the pope as "head of all the churches" in his decree of 533 AD, but actual implementation of papal temporal supremacy began in 538 when the last Arian opposition (Ostrogoths) was broken. - Sources: Siege of Rome (537-538) — Wikipedia; 538 A.D. and the Transition — IJHSS

    1798 AD — End of the 1260-Year Period: - On February 10, 1798, French General Louis-Alexandre Berthier marched into Rome, proclaimed a Roman Republic, and demanded that Pope Pius VI renounce his temporal authority. - On February 20, 1798, Pope Pius VI was formally arrested at the Quirinal Palace and placed under military custody. - Pius VI refused to renounce his temporal authority and was taken prisoner, transported through Italy and France, and died in captivity at Valence on August 29, 1799. - This marked the end of papal temporal power until its partial restoration (the "deadly wound healed" of Rev 13:3). - Sources: When Napoleon's forces kidnapped the Pope — History Skills; Pope Pius VI — Wikipedia; 1798: Pope Captured — History.info

    Focus Areas¶

    1. Rev 12:5 Identification — The Man Child Is Christ: WHAT: Determine whether "a man child, who was to rule all nations with a rod of iron: and her child was caught up unto God, and to his throne" must refer to Christ. WHY: Tool discoveries show PSA 2:9 ("rod of iron") is universally Messianic, REV 19:15 applies the same phrase to Christ, and "caught up unto God, and to his throne" matches Christ's ascension (ACT 1:9, HEB 12:2). The prior study (12-rev12-woman-dragon-child.md) classified this as E (Explicit). This is the anchor that makes the chapter's starting point non-negotiable. HOW: Retrieve full text of REV 12:5 with chapter context, PSA 2:1-12, REV 19:15, REV 2:27, ACT 1:9-11, HEB 12:2. Run cross-testament parallels on REV 12:5 (both OT and NT). Run greek_parser.py on REV 12:5 to examine the grammar of "to rule" (mellei poimainein). Look up G4165 (poimaino, "to shepherd/rule") usage.

    2. Rev 12:1-2 — The Woman as Israel/Church Continuity: WHAT: Identify the woman clothed with the sun, moon under feet, crown of twelve stars. WHY: Tool discoveries show GEN 37:9 (Joseph's dream: sun, moon, eleven stars = Jacob/Israel), ISA 54:5-6 (God as husband of woman/Israel), GAL 4:26 (Jerusalem above = mother of us all), and EPH 5:25-32 (Christ and the church). The woman represents God's covenant people through both testaments. HOW: Retrieve full text of REV 12:1-2, GEN 37:9-10, ISA 54:5-6, GAL 4:26, EPH 5:25-32, ISA 66:7-8. Run greek_parser.py on REV 12:1 for "woman" (gyne G1135) and "wonder/sign" (semeion G4592). Run cross-testament parallels on REV 12:1.

    3. Rev 12:3-4 — The Dragon Identified: WHAT: Examine the great red dragon with seven heads and ten horns, its identification as Satan in 12:9, and its connection to the Genesis 3 serpent. WHY: The Nave's entry for DRAGON explicitly lists REV 12 as symbolical, and the SATAN entry identifies the "great red dragon" (REV 12:3) and "that old serpent" (REV 12:9; 20:2) as Satan's titles. GEN 3:15 connects the serpent to the enmity against the woman's seed. HOW: Retrieve REV 12:3-4, REV 12:9, REV 20:2, GEN 3:1-15. Look up G1404 (drakon) with --verses. Run cross-testament parallels on REV 12:9 (both OT and NT). Examine ISA 27:1 and PSA 74:13 for OT dragon/sea monster imagery.

    4. Rev 12:6 and 12:14 — The 1260 Days = Time, Times, Half a Time: WHAT: Establish that 1260 days (12:6) and "time, times, half a time" (12:14) are the same period, and that this period = DAN 7:25 verbatim. WHY: The time-times-half-time study proved that Rev 12:14 directly quotes the LXX of Dan 7:25. The revs-33 study showed contextual differentiation: 1260 days = preservation vocabulary, time-times-half = Danielic cosmic formula. Seven references across Daniel and Revelation all describe the same 3.5-year prophetic period. HOW: Retrieve full text of REV 12:6, REV 12:14, DAN 7:25, DAN 12:7, REV 11:2-3, REV 13:5. Run greek_parser.py on REV 12:6 and REV 12:14 to show the LXX quotation. Compare the Greek of REV 12:14 with LXX DAN 7:25. Look up G2540 (kairos) usage.

    5. The Day-Year Principle Applied: 1260 Days = 1260 Years: WHAT: Demonstrate the biblical basis for converting prophetic days to literal years, and apply it to the 1260-day period. WHY: hist-03 proved the day-year principle empirically via the 70-weeks prophecy (457 BC + 483 years = AD 27). The OT provides explicit day-for-year analogies (NUM 14:34; EZK 4:6). Daniel's own reaction to 2300 days (Dan 8:27, fainting) proves he understood them as years. HOW: Retrieve NUM 14:34, EZK 4:6, DAN 9:24-25, DAN 8:14, DAN 8:27. Document the mathematical calculation: 1260 prophetic days x day-year principle = 1260 literal years. State the historical dates: 538 AD + 1260 years = 1798 AD.

    6. Rev 12:7-12 — War in Heaven and Satan Cast Down: WHAT: Examine the war in heaven, Michael defeating the dragon, and the timing markers that connect this to Christ's victory. WHY: The revelation-12-timing study established that Rev 12:7-12 describes the expulsion at Christ's victory (not the primordial rebellion of 12:4a). Key evidence: "by the blood of the Lamb" (12:11), "NOW is come salvation" (12:10), John 12:31 ("now shall the prince of this world be cast out"), and Luke 10:18 ("I beheld Satan as lightning fall from heaven"). Satan had heavenly access until the cross (Job 1:6, Zech 3:1). HOW: Retrieve REV 12:7-12, JHN 12:31, LUK 10:18, JOB 1:6-12, ZEC 3:1-2. Run greek_parser.py on REV 12:10-11. Run cross-testament parallels on REV 12:7.

    7. Rev 12:13-16 — Persecution and the Earth Helping the Woman: WHAT: Examine the dragon's persecution of the woman after Christ's ascension, and the earth helping the woman by swallowing the flood. WHY: The PERSECUTION topic in Nave's lists REV 12:11 as persecution of the righteous, and history confirms centuries of persecution during the 1260-year period. The "earth helped the woman" (12:16) and "two wings of a great eagle" (12:14) echo Exodus 19:4 (the revs-33 study identified this as an OT allusion, AN075). HOW: Retrieve REV 12:13-16, EXO 19:4. Run cross-testament parallels on REV 12:14 and REV 12:16. Investigate historical sources (Gibbon, Schaff, Foxe) for documentation of medieval persecution.

    8. Rev 12:17 — The Post-1260 Remnant: WHAT: Analyze the dual identification marks of the remnant: "keep the commandments of God" and "have the testimony of Jesus Christ." WHY: The law-28 study proved "commandments of God" (entole G1785 with definite article) = Decalogue in 43/43 NT instances. Rev 19:10 defines "testimony of Jesus" = "spirit of prophecy." Rev 14:12 uses the same dual formula. The remnant exists AFTER the 1260-year period. HOW: Retrieve REV 12:17, REV 14:12, REV 19:10, 1JN 5:3, JHN 14:15, ECC 12:13-14.
