    Cross-Testament Parallels Raw Data¶

    REV 1:19 (Temporal three-part division)¶

    OT: Weak matches — no strong OT parallel for the three-part temporal formula. Isa 30:8 (write), 2Chr 34:31 (after/place/write) are closest. NT: MRK 13:4 (0.333) — "when shall these things be?" | REV 4:1 (0.329) — "things which must be hereafter" (same phrase meta tauta) | LUK 1:1 (0.312) — "those things which are most surely believed among us" KEY: Rev 4:1 uses the same phrase "meta tauta" connecting to Rev 1:19's "things which shall be hereafter."

    REV 2:7 (Tree of life — overcomer #1)¶

    OT: Gen 3:8 (0.380) — hear/tree | Gen 2:16 (0.379) — eat/tree | Isa 55:3 (0.414) — ear/hear | 2Ki 19:16 (0.420) — ear/hear NT: REV 2:17 (0.704), REV 2:11 (0.697), REV 2:29/3:6/3:13/3:22 (0.663) — all ear-formula parallels | MAT 13:43 (0.496) — "He that hath ears, let him hear" | MAT 11:15 (0.456) — same ear formula KEY: Genesis 2-3 tree of life connection. Synoptic ear formula (Mat 11:15; 13:9,43) is the closest non-Revelation parallel to the seven-church ear formula.

    REV 2:11 (Second death — overcomer #2)¶

    OT: Job 28:22 (0.394) — death/ear/hear | Jer 26:11 (0.390) — death/ear/hear NT: All other ear-formula verses in Rev 2-3 (0.63-0.70) KEY: "Second death" (thanatos deuteros) is a concept unique to Revelation (2:11; 20:6,14; 21:8).

    REV 2:14 (Balaam doctrine)¶

    OT: Num 23:27 (0.339) — Balak/there | Jos 24:9 (0.325) — Balak | 1Ki 21:26 (0.310) — idols NT: REV 2:20 (0.446) — Jezebel parallel (eat/idol/immorality) | ACT 15:20 (0.351) — idol/immorality | ACT 21:25 (0.346) — idol/immorality/things | 1CO 8:10 (0.331) — eat/idol KEY: Rev 2:14 and 2:20 parallel each other (0.446) — both involve teaching to eat things sacrificed to idols and commit immorality. The Acts 15 decree (15:20,29) addresses the same issues.

    REV 2:17 (Hidden manna, white stone, new name — overcomer #3)¶

    OT: Ezk 11:19 (0.400) — new/stone | Zech 3:9 (0.374) — stone | Ezk 36:26 (0.374) — new/stone NT: REV 2:7 (0.704) — full ear formula | other ear-formula verses (0.62-0.66) KEY: OT "new stone" parallels in Ezekiel's new heart prophecies.

    REV 2:20 (Jezebel)¶

    OT: 1Ki 21:7 (0.371) — eat/Jezebel/woman | 1Ki 21:5 (0.371) — eat/Jezebel/woman | 1Ki 16:31 (0.347) — Jezebel/woman | Jdg 4:4 (0.340) — prophetess/woman NT: REV 2:14 (0.446) — parallel teaching (eat/idol/immorality) | ACT 15:20 (0.362) — idol/immorality | REV 19:2 (0.349) — immorality/servant KEY: 1 Kings Jezebel connection confirmed: same name, same pattern of leading God's people into idolatry and immorality. The literary typology is intentional.

    REV 2:25 ("Till I come")¶

    OT: Dan 7:22 (0.330) — time/until | Dan 7:25 (0.326) — time/until NT: HEB 10:37 (0.373) — "he that shall come will come" | REV 3:11 (0.361) — hold fast | 1CO 15:2 (0.346) — hold fast KEY: Daniel 7 parallel (time/until) connects to prophetic time periods. Heb 10:37 is Second Coming text.

    REV 2:26 (Rod of iron, power over nations — overcomer #4)¶

    OT: Weak OT matches in parallel tool, but Psalm 2:8-9 is the obvious source (rod of iron, potter's vessel language directly quoted in Rev 2:27). NT: 1CO 15:24 (0.440) — authority/end/over | REV 3:21 (0.425) — overcome | LUK 1:33 (0.377) — end/over KEY: Rev 2:27 directly quotes Psalm 2:9. The overcomer shares in Christ's Messianic authority.

    REV 3:3 (Come as a thief)¶

    OT: Weak matches. NT: REV 2:5 (0.443) — remember/repent/therefore | REV 16:15 (0.436) — thief/watch | MAT 24:42 (0.414) — know/therefore/watch | 1TH 5:2 (0.390) — know/thief | MAT 25:13 (0.365) — hour/know/watch KEY: "Thief" imagery parallels: 1 Thess 5:2 ("day of the Lord so cometh as a thief"), 2 Pet 3:10, Rev 16:15. These are consistently Second Coming texts.

    REV 3:5 (White raiment, book of life — overcomer #5)¶

    OT: Moderate matches. NT: MAT 10:32 (0.440) — before/confess/father | LUK 12:8 (0.437) — angel/before/confess | REV 2:17 (0.430) — name/overcome/white | REV 3:12 (0.415) — name/overcome | REV 13:8 (0.392) — book/name KEY: "Confess his name before my Father" parallels Mat 10:32 / Luk 12:8 — same promise, same language.

    REV 3:7 (Key of David)¶

    OT: 2Sa 11:23 (0.410) — David/messenger/open | ISA 22:22 (0.399) — David/key/open | Zech 13:1 (0.364) — David/open NT: REV 2:12/2:18/3:1 (0.43-0.45) — parallel church introduction formulas | REV 5:5 (0.364) — David/open KEY: Isaiah 22:22 confirmed as direct source: "the key of the house of David...he shall open, and none shall shut." Rev 5:5 also connects (Root of David who prevailed to open).

    REV 3:12 (Pillar in temple, new name, new Jerusalem — overcomer #6)¶

    OT: Deut 29:19 (0.391) — heaven/name/write | Zeph 1:4 (0.381) — Jerusalem/name | Isa 4:3 (0.358) — Jerusalem/write NT: REV 21:2 (0.458) — city/heaven/Jerusalem | REV 3:21 (0.442) — overcome | REV 21:10 (0.415) — city/heaven/Jerusalem | REV 2:17 (0.409) — name/new/overcome KEY: Rev 21:2 is the strongest NT parallel — direct fulfillment of the new Jerusalem promise. The overcomer becomes a permanent fixture ("pillar") in God's presence.

    REV 3:14 (Faithful and true witness, beginning of creation)¶

    OT: Moderate matches. NT: REV 2:8/2:18/3:7 (0.42-0.45) — parallel church introductions | 2PE 3:4 (0.411) — beginning/creation | REV 1:5 (0.397) — faithful/witness | REV 21:5 (0.396) — faithful/true | COL 4:16 (0.389) — church/Laodicea KEY: Rev 1:5 confirmed as source for "faithful and true witness" self-description.

    REV 3:20 (I stand at the door and knock)¶

    OT: Jdg 19:27 (0.351) — door/open | 1Ki 19:13 (0.333) — hear/open/stand | Song 5:5 (0.328) — open NT: LUK 13:25 (0.429) — door/knock/open/stand | JHN 10:9 (0.398) — door | REV 4:1 (0.390) — door/open/voice | JHN 10:3 (0.376) — hear/open/voice KEY: Luk 13:25 is the strongest parallel — the master knocking at the door. Song 5:2-5 (beloved knocking) is also relevant. The door imagery is consistently eschatological in NT: Mat 24:33; Jas 5:9; Mrk 13:29.

    REV 3:21 (Sit on throne — overcomer #7)¶

    OT: Zech 6:13 (0.350) — sit/dominion | 1Ki 1:21 (0.349) — father NT: REV 3:12 (0.442) — overcome | REV 2:26 (0.425) — overcome | LUK 22:29 (0.401) — dominion/father | MAT 25:34 (0.396) — dominion/father | MAT 19:28 (0.368) — sit/dominion KEY: Luk 22:29 ("I appoint unto you a kingdom, as my Father hath appointed unto me") is the closest synoptic parallel. Mat 19:28 ("sit upon twelve thrones") also relevant.

    REV 21:7 (Overcomer inherits all things)¶

    OT: 1Chr 22:11 (0.346) — weak matches. NT: REV 3:12 (0.405), REV 3:21 (0.403) — other overcomer promises | 2CO 6:18 (0.376) — "I will be a Father unto you, and ye shall be my sons" | 1JN 5:5 (0.374) — overcome | MAT 25:34 (0.371) — inherit KEY: 2 Cor 6:18 parallels the sonship language. Mat 25:34 parallels the inheritance language.

    REV 2:4 (Left first love)¶

    OT: Psa 109:17 (0.322) — compassion | Psa 109:4 (0.321) — compassion | Pro 12:1 (0.321) — compassion | Psa 97:10 (0.311) — compassion NT: JHN 5:42 (0.393) — compassion/love | JHN 15:9 (0.382) — compassion/love | 2CO 11:11 (0.365) — compassion/love | LUK 6:27 (0.356) — compassion/love | 1JN 3:11 (0.353) — compassion/love KEY: OT parallels are weak (no strong lexical match for "left first love"). NT parallels center on Johannine love passages — John 5:42 ("ye have not the love of God in you") is the closest conceptual match. Jeremiah 2:2 ("the kindness of thy youth, the love of thine espousals") is the classic OT parallel not picked up by the tool.

    REV 2:10 (Ten days tribulation, crown of life)¶

    OT: 2Sa 12:18 (0.377) — alive/behold/fear | Deu 30:19 (0.370) — alive/death | Num 35:32 (0.346) — death/unto | Ecc 8:13 (0.344) — fear NT: 1Jn 5:16 (0.387) — alive/death | REV 2:22 (0.386) — behold/cast/tribulation | 1PE 5:8 (0.385) — about/devil | JAS 1:12 (0.375) — alive/crown | ROM 6:22 (0.370) — alive/unto KEY: James 1:12 ("he shall receive the crown of life, which the Lord hath promised to them that love him") is the direct verbal parallel to Rev 2:10's "crown of life" promise. 1 Pet 5:8 ("your adversary the devil") parallels the devil casting believers into prison. Daniel 1:12-15 (ten days' trial) is the classic OT parallel not captured by the tool.

    REV 2:27 (Rod of iron — Psalm 2:9 quotation)¶

    OT: PSA 2:9 (0.439) — iron/potter/rod | Jer 18:3 (0.353) — potter | Jos 17:18 (0.347) — iron | Jer 18:4 (0.346) — potter | Psa 149:8 (0.339) — iron NT: REV 19:15 (0.467) — iron/rod/shepherd | REV 12:5 (0.333) — iron/rod | 2Ti 4:14 (0.306) — weak | Rom 9:21 (0.301) — potter KEY: Psalm 2:9 confirmed as the direct OT source (highest OT match at 0.439): "Thou shalt break them with a rod of iron; thou shalt dash them in pieces like a potter's vessel." Rev 19:15 is the strongest NT parallel (0.467): Christ Himself fulfilling the same Psalm 2 prophecy at His return. Rev 12:5 (man child ruling with rod of iron) completes the chain: Psalm 2:9 -> Rev 2:27 (overcomer shares the authority) -> Rev 12:5 (man child) -> Rev 19:15 (Christ executes it).
