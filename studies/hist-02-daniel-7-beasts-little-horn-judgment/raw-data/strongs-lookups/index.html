    Raw Strong's Lookup Data¶

    H1780 -- din (Aramaic judgment)¶

    H1780 - 5 total occurrences, 4 unique translations: 2 ( 40.0%) judgment 1 ( 20.0%) the judgment 1 ( 20.0%) and judgment 1 ( 20.0%) But the judgment

    Lexicon: דִּין (dîyn), masculine noun. (Aramaic) corresponding to H1779: judgment. BDB: absolute ד׳ Daniel 4:34 = justice; emphatic דִּינָה Ezra 7:26 "judgment be executed upon him"; דִּינָא = judges, court Daniel 7:10, 26; Daniel 7:22.

    Verses as "judgment": Ezr 7:26, Dan 4:37 Verses as "the judgment": Dan 7:10

    H6268 -- attiyq (Aramaic: ancient/advanced)¶

    H6268 - 3 total occurrences, 2 unique translations: 2 ( 66.7%) Ancient 1 ( 33.3%) and the Ancient

    Lexicon: עַתִּיק (ʻattîyq), adjective. Advanced, aged. Construct (מַיָּא-) יוֺמִין ע׳ "one advanced, aged, in days" Dan 7:9, 13, 22.

    Verses as "Ancient": Dan 7:13, Dan 7:22

    H6267 -- attiyq (Hebrew cognate: ancient/drawn)¶

    H6267 - 2 total occurrences, 2 unique translations: 1 ( 50.0%) ancient 1 ( 50.0%) drawn

    Lexicon: עַתִּיק (ʻattîyq), adjective. From H6275; removed, i.e. weaned; also antique. 1 Chr 4:22, Isa 28:9.

    H7162 -- qeren (Aramaic: horn)¶

    H7162 - 21 total occurrences, 11 unique translations: 4 ( 19.0%) of the cornet 4 ( 19.0%) horns 4 ( 19.0%) horn [other translations: the, and, the horns, in, the horn, of, and the, And the]

    Lexicon: קֶרֶן (qeren), feminine noun. (Aramaic) horn. 1. instrument of music, Dan 3:5,7,10,15. 2. symbolic in vision, Dan 7:7,8(3x),11,20(2x),21,24.

    H317 -- ochori (Aramaic: another/sequential)¶

    H317 - 6 total occurrences, 2 unique translations: 5 ( 83.3%) another 1 ( 16.7%) the other

    Lexicon: אָחֳרִי (ʼochŏrîy), adjective feminine. Another. Dan 2:39(2x), 7:5, 6, 8, 20. For masculine form see H321 אָחֳרָן.

    H2191 -- ze'iyr (Aramaic: little/small)¶

    H2191 - 5 total occurrences, 1 unique translation: 5 (100.0%) a little

    Lexicon: זְעֵיר (zᵉʻêyr), masculine noun. From unused root meaning to dwindle; small, little.

    H5732 -- iddan (Aramaic: time/year)¶

    H5732 - 15 total occurrences, 9 unique translations: 4 ( 26.7%) times 2 ( 13.3%) the time 2 ( 13.3%) at 2 ( 13.3%) time 1 each: the times, and time, a time, and times, of time

    Lexicon: עִדָּן (ʻiddân), masculine noun. A set time; technically, a year. 1. general: time as duration Dan 2:8; 7:12. 2. definite time = year: שִׁבְעָה ע׳ = seven years Dan 4:13,20,22,29; עִדָּן וְעִדָּנִין וּפְלַג עִדָּן Dan 7:25 (i.e. 3½ years).

    Verses as "times": Dan 4:16, 4:23, 4:25, 4:32 Verses as "a time": Dan 7:25

    H3118 -- yom (Aramaic: day)¶

    H3118 - 20 total occurrences, 10 unique translations: 4 ( 20.0%) the 3 ( 15.0%) days 3 ( 15.0%) of days 2 ( 10.0%) time, day, a day 1 each: by day, And in the days, of the days, and in the days

    Lexicon: יוֹם (yôwm), masculine noun. (Aramaic) day. עַתִּיק י׳ "one aged of days" Dan 7:9,13,22; עָֽלְמָא י׳ "days of old" Ezra 4:15,19; בְּאַחֲרִית י׳ "in the latter part of the days" Dan 2:28.

    H8199 -- shaphat (Hebrew: to judge)¶

    H8199 - 220 total occurrences, 122 unique translations: Major: judge(13), the judge(9), judged(9), to judge(8), judges(8), shall judge(6), the judges(6)

    Lexicon: שָׁפַט (shâphaṭ), verb. To judge, i.e. pronounce sentence (for or against); to vindicate or punish; to govern. Range: avenge, condemn, contend, defend, execute (judgment), judge, plead, reason, rule.

    G2362 -- thronos (Greek: throne)¶

    G2362 - 61 total occurrences, 7 unique translations: 47 ( 77.0%) throne 4 ( 6.6%) thrones 3 ( 4.9%) seats 3 ( 4.9%) seat 2 ( 3.3%) the throne 1 each: a throne, the seats

    Lexicon: θρόνος (thrónos), masculine noun. From θράω (to sit); a stately seat ("throne"); by implication, power.

    Verses as "throne" (47): Mat 5:34; 23:22; Luk 1:32; Act 2:30; 7:49; Heb 1:8; 4:16; 8:1; 12:2; Rev 1:4; 3:21(2x); 4:2,3,4,5(2x),6(3x),9,10(2x); 5:1,6,7,11,13; 6:16; 7:9,10,11(2x),15(2x),17; 8:3; 12:5; 14:3,5; 16:17; 19:4,5; 20:11; 21:5; 22:1,3.

    G988 -- blasphemia (Greek: blasphemy)¶

    G988 - 18 total occurrences, 7 unique translations: 7 ( 38.9%) blasphemy 5 ( 27.8%) blasphemies 2 ( 11.1%) of blasphemy 1 each: the blasphemy, evil speaking, railings, a railing

    Lexicon: βλασφημία (blasphēmía), feminine noun. Vilification (especially against God): blasphemy, evil speaking.

    Verses as "blasphemy": Mat 12:31; 26:65; Mar 7:22; 14:64; Col 3:8; Rev 2:9; 13:6.

    G2768 -- keras (Greek: horn)¶

    G2768 - 11 total occurrences, 2 unique translations: 10 ( 90.9%) horns 1 ( 9.1%) an horn

    Lexicon: κέρας (kéras), neuter noun. A horn.

    Verses as "horns": Rev 5:6; 9:13; 12:3; 13:1(2x); 13:11; 17:3,7,12,16.

    G2920 -- krisis (Greek: judgment/decision)¶

    G2920 - 34 total occurrences, 6 unique translations: 18 ( 52.9%) judgment 8 ( 23.5%) of judgment 2 each: damnation, the judgment, accusation, judgments

    Lexicon: κρίσις (krísis), feminine noun. Decision (for or against); a tribunal; justice.

    LXX connection: Used for Dan 7:10,26 (דִּין). NT: Rev 14:7; 18:10; 19:2.
