    H2891 — taher¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: taher
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 94 (115 total)
    • Definition: to be bright; to be pure (Levitically, uncontaminated; morally, innocent or holy): clean, cleanse, purge, purify.

    Key Translations (115 total)¶

    • 6 (5.2%) "of him that is to be cleansed"
    • 5 (4.3%) "and be clean"
    • 5 (4.3%) "clean"
    • 4 (3.5%) "shall pronounce him clean"
    • 3 (2.6%) "to cleanse"
    • Heavy concentration in Leviticus 13-14 (leprosy cleansing) and Lev 16 (DOA)

    H3722 — kaphar¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: kaphar
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 102
    • Definition: to cover; figuratively, to expiate or condone, to placate or cancel: atonement, cleanse, forgive, reconcile.
    • Massively concentrated in Leviticus (especially Lev 16)
    • Used in Dan 9:24: "to make reconciliation [lekhapper] for iniquity"

    H6944 — qodesh¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: qodesh
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 468
    • Definition: sacred place or thing; sanctity: holiness, holy, sanctuary.
    • Used in Dan 8:13,14 (sanctuary); Dan 9:16,20,24 (holy city, holy mountain, most Holy)

    • Definition: from H6150; dusk: day, evening, night.

    H1242 — boqer (morning)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: boqer
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 205
    • Definition: from H1239; dawn; morning.

    H6588 — pesha (transgression)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: pesha
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 93
    • Definition: from H6586; a revolt (national, moral or religious): rebellion, sin, transgression.

    Verses where H6588 = "transgression" (7 occurrences)¶

    H6664 — tsedeq (righteousness)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: tsedeq
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 116
    • Definition: from H6663; the right; equity: just, justice, righteous, righteousness.
    • Used in Dan 9:24: "everlasting righteousness" (tsedeq olamim)

    Verses where H6664 = "righteousness" (27 occurrences)¶

    Including Dan 9:24; Isa 45:8,19; 51:1,7; Psa 85:10; 97:2; 119:142,172; etc.

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: dikaioo
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 40 (43 total)
    • Definition: from G1342; to render just or innocent: free, justify.

    All Translations (43 occurrences)¶

    • 7 (16.3%) "justified"
    • 4 (9.3%) "is justified"
    • 3 (7.0%) "be justified"
    • 2 (4.7%) "are justified"
    • 2 (4.7%) "we might be justified"

    Verses where G1344 = "justified" (7 occurrences)¶

    G2511 — katharizo (cleanse)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: katharizo
    • POS: verb
    • BLB Count: 30
    • Definition: from G2513; to cleanse: clean, purge, purify.
    • Used in Heb 9:22-23 (purification of heavenly things)
    • Anomalously used in LXX Dan 8:14 instead of dikaioo

    H3117 — yom (day)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: yom
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 2,000+
    • Definition: from unused root meaning to be hot; a day; figuratively, a space of time.
    • Daniel uses yom/yamim in Dan 8:26; 12:11,12,13 — but NOT in 8:14

    H4941 — mishpat (judgment/verdict)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: mishpat
    • POS: masculine noun
    • BLB Count: 421
    • Definition: from H8199; a verdict pronounced judicially; justice; right.

    H3725 — kippur (atonement)¶

    Lexicon¶

    • Transliteration: kippur
    • POS: masculine plural noun
    • BLB Count: 8
    • Definition: from H3722; expiation (only in plural): atonement.
    • Only in Exo 29:36; 30:10,16; Lev 23:27,28; 25:9; Num 5:8; 29:11
